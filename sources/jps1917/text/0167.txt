{{dropinitial|{{anchor|21:1}}21}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto Moses: Speak unto the priests the sons of Aaron, and say unto them:

There shall none defile himself for the dead among his people; {{verse|chapter=21|verse=2}}except for his kin, that is near unto him, for his mother, and for his father, and for his son, and for his daughter, and for his brother; {{verse|chapter=21|verse=3}}and for his sister a virgin, that is near unto him, that hath had no husband, for her may he defile himself. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=4}}He shall not defile himself, being a chief man among his people, to profane himself. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=5}}They shall not make baldness upon their head, neither shall they shave off the corners of their beard, nor make any cuttings in their flesh. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=6}}They shall be holy unto their God, and not profane the name of their God; for the offerings of the {{sc|Lord}} made by fire, the bread of their God, they do offer; therefore they shall be holy. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=7}}They shall not take a woman that is a harlot, or profaned; neither shall they take a woman put away from her husband; for he is holy unto his God. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=8}}Thou shalt sanctify him therefore; for he offereth the bread of thy God; he shall be holy unto thee; for I the {{sc|Lord}}, who sanctify you, am holy. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=9}}And the daughter of any priest, if she profane herself by playing the harlot, she profaneth her father: she shall be burnt with fire.

{{verse|chapter=21|verse=10}}And the priest that is highest among his brethren, upon whose head the anointing oil is poured, and that is consecrated to put on the garments, shall not let the hair of his head go loose, nor rend his clothes; {{verse|chapter=21|verse=11}}neither shall he go in to any dead body, nor defile himself for his father, or for his mother; {{verse|chapter=21|verse=12}}neither shall he go out of the sanctuary, nor profane the sanctuary of his God; for the consecration of the anointing oil of his God is upon him: I am the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=13}}And he shall take a wife in her virginity. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=14}}A widow, or one divorced, or a profaned woman, or a harlot, these shall he not take; but a virgin of his own people shall he take to wife. {{verse|chapter=21|verse=15}}And he shall not profane his seed among his people; for I am the {{sc|Lord}} who sanctify him.
