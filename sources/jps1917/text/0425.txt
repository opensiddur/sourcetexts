{{verse|1|49}}And all the guests of Adonijah were afraid, and rose up, and went every man his way. {{verse|1|50}}And Adonijah feared because of Solomon; and he arose, and went, and caught hold on the horns of the altar. {{verse|1|51}}And it was told Solomon, saying: 'Behold, Adonijah feareth king Solomon; for, lo, he hath laid hold on the horns of the altar, saying: Let king Solomon swear unto me first of all that he will not slay his servant with the sword.' {{verse|1|52}}And Solomon said: 'If he shall show himself a worthy man, there shall not a hair of him fall to the earth; but if wickedness be found in him, he shall die.' {{verse|1|53}}So king Solomon sent, and they brought him down from the altar. And he came and prostrated himself before king Solomon; and Solomon said unto him: 'Go to thy house.'
