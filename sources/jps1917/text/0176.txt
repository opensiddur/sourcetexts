for I am the {{sc|Lord}} their God. {{verse|chapter=26|verse=45}}But I will for their sakes remember the covenant of their ancestors, whom I brought forth out of the land of Egypt in the sight of the nations, that I might be their God: I am the {{sc|Lord}}.
