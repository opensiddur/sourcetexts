<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|2}}.22|FIRST CHRONICLES|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>Jair, who had three and twenty cities in the land of Gilead. {{verse|2|23}}And Geshur and Aram took Havvoth-jair from them, with Kenath, and the villages thereof, even threescore cities. All these were the sons of Machir the father of Gilead. {{verse|2|24}}And after that Hezron was dead in Caleb-ephrath, then Abiah Hezron's wife bore him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.

{{verse|2|25}}And the sons of Jerahmeel the first-born of Hezron were Ram the first-born, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, Ahijah. {{verse|2|26}}And Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.

{{verse|2|27}}And the sons of Ram the first-born of Jerahmeel were Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker. {{verse|2|28}}And the sons of Onam were Shammai, and Jada; and the sons of Shammai: Nadab, and Abishur. {{verse|2|29}}And the name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail; and she bore him Ahban, and Molid. {{verse|2|30}}And the sons of Nadab: Seled, and Appaim; but Seled died without children. {{verse|2|31}}And the sons of Appaim: Ishi. And the sons of Ishi: Sheshan. And the sons of Sheshan: Ahlai. {{verse|2|32}}And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai: Jether, and Jonathan: and Jether died without children. {{verse|2|33}}And the sons of Jonathan: Peleth, and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.

{{verse|2|34}}Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha. {{verse|2|35}}So Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bore him Attai. {{verse|2|36}}And Attai begot Nathan, and Nathan begot Zabad; {{verse|2|37}}and Zabad begot Ephlal, and Ephlal begot Obed; {{verse|2|38}}and Obed begot Jehu, and Jehu begot Azariah; {{verse|2|39}}and Azariah begot Helez, and Helez begot Eleasah; {{verse|2|40}}and Eleasah begot Sisamai, and Sisamai begot Shallum; {{verse|2|41}}and Shallum begot Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begot Elishama.

{{verse|2|42}}And the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were Mesha his first born, who was the father of Ziph, and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron. {{verse|2|43}}And the sons of Hebron: Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema. {{verse|2|44}}And Shema begot Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem begot Shammai. {{verse|2|45}}And the son of Shammai was Maon; and Maon was the father of Beth-zur. {{verse|2|46}}And Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bore Haran, and Moza, and Gazez; and Haran begot Gazez. {{verse|2|47}}And the sons of Jahdai: Regem, and Jotham, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph. {{verse|2|48}}Maacah, Caleb's concubine, bore Sheber and Tirhanah. {{verse|2|49}}And [the wife of] Shaaph the father of Madmannah bore Sheva the father of Machbenah and the father of Gibea. And the daughter of Caleb was Achsah. {{verse|2|50}}These were the sons of Caleb.

The sons of Hur the first-born of Ephrath: Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim; {{verse|2|51}}Salma the father of Beth-lehem, Hareph the father of Beth-gader. {{verse|2|52}}And Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim had sons: Haroeh, and half of the Menuhoth. {{verse|2|53}}And the families of Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites, and the Puthites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zorathites, and the Eshtaolites.

{{verse|2|54}}The sons of Salma: Beth-lehem, and the Netophathites, Atroth-bethjoab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites. {{verse|2|55}}And the families of scribes that dwelt at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, the Sucathites. These are the Kenites that came of Hammath, the father of the house of Rechab.
