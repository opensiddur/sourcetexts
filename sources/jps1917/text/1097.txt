{{dropinitial|{{anchor|14:1}}14}} And Huram king of Tyre sent messengers to David, and cedar-trees, and masons, and carpenters, to build him a house. {{verse|14|2}}And David perceived that the {{sc|Lord}} had established him king over Israel, for his kingdom was exalted exceedingly, for His people Israel's sake.

{{verse|14|3}}And David took more wives at Jerusalem; and David begot more sons and daughters. {{verse|14|4}}And these are the names of the children whom he had in Jerusalem: Shammua, and Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon; {{verse|14|5}}and Ibhar, and Elishua, and Elpelet; {{verse|14|6}}and Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia; {{verse|14|7}}and Elishama, and Beeliada, and Eliphelet.

{{verse|14|8}}And when the Philistines heard that David was anointed king over all Israel, all the Philistines went up to seek David; and David heard of it, and went out to meet them. {{verse|14|9}}Now the Philistines had come and made a raid in the valley of Rephaim. {{verse|14|10}}And David inquired of God, saying: 'Shall I go up against the Philistines? and wilt Thou deliver them into my hand?' And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto him: 'Go up; for I will deliver them into thy hand.' {{verse|14|11}}So they came up to Baal-perazim, and David smote them there; and David said: 'God hath broken mine enemies by my hand, like the breach of waters.' Therefore they called the name of that place <ref>That is, ''The possessor of breaches.''</ref>Baal-perazim. {{verse|14|12}}And they left their gods there; and David gave commandment, and they were burned with fire.

{{verse|14|13}}And the Philistines yet again made a raid in the valley. {{verse|14|14}}And David inquired again of God; and God said unto him: 'Thou shalt not go up after them; turn away from them, and come upon them over against the mulberry-trees. {{verse|14|15}}And it shall be, when thou hearest the sound of marching in the tops of the mulberry-trees, that then thou shalt go out to battle; for God is gone out before thee to smite the host of the Philistines.' {{verse|14|16}}And David did as God commanded him; and they smote the host of the Philistines from Gibeon even to Gezer. {{verse|14|17}}And the fame of David went out into all lands; and the {{sc|Lord}} brought the fear of him upon all nations.
