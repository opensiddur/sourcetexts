:Set me on high from them that rise up against me.
:{{verse|59|3|align=float}}Deliver me from the workers of iniquity,

:{{verse|59|4|align=float}}For, lo, they lie in wait for my soul;
:The impudent gather themselves together against me;

:Awake Thou to help me, and behold.
:{{verse|59|6|align=float}}Thou therefore, O {{sc|Lord}} God of hosts, the God of Israel,

:Show no mercy to any iniquitous traitors. {{float right|Selah}}
