<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|36}}.1|JOB|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{dropinitial|{{anchor|36:1}}36}} Elihu also proceeded, and said:

:{{verse|36|2|align=float}}Suffer me a little, and I will tell thee;
:For there are yet words on God's behalf.
:{{verse|36|3|align=float}}I will fetch my knowledge from afar,
:And will ascribe righteousness to my Maker.
:{{verse|36|4|align=float}}For truly my words are not false;
:One that is upright in mind is with thee.
:{{verse|36|5|align=float}}Behold, God is mighty, yet He despiseth not any;
:He is mighty in strength of understanding.
:{{verse|36|6|align=float}}He preserveth not the life of the wicked;
:But giveth to the poor their right.
:{{verse|36|7|align=float}}He withdraweth not His eyes from the righteous;
:But with kings upon the throne
:He setteth them for ever, and they are exalted.
:{{verse|36|8|align=float}}And if they be bound in fetters,
:And be holden in cords of affliction;
:{{verse|36|9|align=float}}Then He declareth unto them their work,
:And their transgressions, that they have behaved themselves proudly.
:{{verse|36|10|align=float}}He openeth also their ear to discipline,
:And commandeth that they return from iniquity.
:{{verse|36|11|align=float}}If they hearken and serve Him,
:They shall spend their days in prosperity,
:And their years in pleasures.
:{{verse|36|12|align=float}}But if they hearken not, they shall perish by the sword,
:And they shall die without knowledge.
:{{verse|36|13|align=float}}But they that are godless in heart lay up anger;
:They cry not for help when He bindeth them.
:{{verse|36|14|align=float}}Their soul perisheth in youth,
:And their life as that of the depraved.
:{{verse|36|15|align=float}}He delivereth the afflicted by His affliction,
:And openeth their ear by tribulation.
:{{verse|36|16|align=float}}Yea, He hath allured thee out of distress
:Into a broad place, where there is no straitness;
:And that which is set on thy table is full of fatness;
:{{verse|36|17|align=float}}And thou art full of the judgment of the wicked;
:Judgment and justice take hold on them.
:{{verse|36|18|align=float}}For beware of wrath, lest thou be led away by thy sufficiency;
:Neither let the greatness of the ransom turn thee aside.
:{{verse|36|19|align=float}}Will thy riches avail, that are without stint,
:Or all the forces of thy strength?
:{{verse|36|20|align=float}}Desire not the night,
:When peoples are cut off in their place.
:{{verse|36|21|align=float}}Take heed, regard not iniquity;
:For this hast thou chosen rather than affliction.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|36|22|align=float}}Behold, God doeth loftily in His power;
:Who is a teacher like Him?
:{{verse|36|23|align=float}}Who hath enjoined Him His way?
:Or who hath said: 'Thou hast wrought unrighteousness'?
:{{verse|36|24|align=float}}Remember that thou magnify His work,
:Whereof men have sung.
:{{verse|36|25|align=float}}All men have looked thereon;
:Man beholdeth it afar off.
:{{verse|36|26|align=float}}Behold, God is great, beyond our knowledge;
:The number of His years is unsearchable.
