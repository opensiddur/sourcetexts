:Nor stood in the way of sinners,

:{{verse|1|2|align=float}}But his delight is in the law of the {{sc|Lord}};
:And in His law doth he meditate day and night.
:{{verse|1|3|align=float}}And he shall be like a tree planted by streams of water,
