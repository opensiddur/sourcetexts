{{verse|5|17}}And when the Philistines heard that David was anointed king over Israel, all the Philistines went up to seek David; and David heard of it, and went down to the hold. {{verse|5|18}}Now the Philistines had come and spread themselves in the valley of Rephaim. {{verse|5|19}}And David inquired of the {{sc|Lord}}, saying: 'Shall I go up against the Philistines? wilt Thou deliver them into my hand?' And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto David: 'Go up; for I will certainly deliver the Philistines into thy hand.' {{verse|5|20}}And David came to Baal-perazim, and David smote them there; and he said: 'The {{sc|Lord}} hath broken mine enemies before me, like the breach of waters.' Therefore the name of that place was called <ref>That is, ''The possessor of breaches.''</ref>Baal-perazim. {{verse|5|21}}And they left their images there, and David and his men took them away.

{{verse|5|22}}And the Philistines came up yet again, and spread themselves in the valley of Rephaim. {{verse|5|23}}And when David inquired of the {{sc|Lord}}, He said: 'Thou shalt not go up; make a circuit behind them, and come upon them over against the mulberry-trees. {{verse|5|24}}And it shall be, when thou hearest the sound of marching in the tops of the mulberry-trees, that then thou shalt bestir thyself; for then is the {{sc|Lord}} gone out before thee to smite the host of the Philistines.' {{verse|5|25}}And David did so, as the {{sc|Lord}} commanded him, and smote the Philistines from Geba until thou come to Gezer.

{{dropinitial|{{anchor|6:1}}6}}And David again gathered together all the chosen men of Israel, thirty thousand. {{verse|6|2}}And David arose, and went with all the people that were with him, from Baale-judah, to bring up from thence the ark of God, whereupon is called the Name, even the name of the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts that sitteth upon the cherubim. {{verse|6|3}}And they set the ark of God upon a new cart, and brought it out of the house of Abinadab that was in the hill; and Uzzah and Ahio, the sons of Abinadab, drove the new cart. {{verse|6|4}}And they brought it out of the house of Abinadab, which was in the hill, with the ark of God, and Ahio went before the ark. {{verse|6|5}}And David and all the house of Israel played before the {{sc|Lord}} with all manner of instruments made of cypress-wood, and with harps, and with psalteries, and with timbrels, and with sistra, and with cymbals.
