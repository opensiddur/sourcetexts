{{verse|chapter=9|verse=28}}And Noah lived after the flood three hundred and fifty years. {{verse|chapter=9|verse=29}}And all the days of Noah were nine hundred and fifty years; and he died.


{{dropinitial|{{anchor|10:1}}10}}{{sc|Now}} these are the generations of the sons of Noah: Shem, Ham, and Japheth; and unto them were sons born after the flood.

{{verse|chapter=10|verse=2}}The sons of Japheth: Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=3}}And the sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=4}}And the sons of Javan: Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=5}}Of these were the isles of the nations divided in their lands, every one after his tongue, after their families, in their nations.

{{verse|chapter=10|verse=6}}And the sons of Ham: Cush, and Mizraim, and Put, and Canaan. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=7}}And the sons of Cush: Seba, and Havilah, and Sabtah, and Raamah, and Sabteca; and the sons of Raamah: Sheba, and Dedan. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=8}}And Cush begot Nimrod; he began to be a mighty one in the earth. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=9}}He was a mighty hunter before the {{sc|Lord}}; wherefore it is said: 'Like Nimrod a mighty hunter before the {{sc|Lord}}.' {{verse|chapter=10|verse=10}}And the beginning of his kingdom was Babel, and Erech, and Accad, and Calneh, in the land of Shinar. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=11}}Out of that land went forth Asshur, and builded Nineveh, and Rehoboth-ir, and Calah, {{verse|chapter=10|verse=12}}and Resen between Nineveh and Calah—the same is the great city. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=13}}And Mizraim begot Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim, {{verse|chapter=10|verse=14}}and Pathrusim, and Casluhim—whence went forth the Philistines—and Caphtorim.

{{verse|chapter=10|verse=15}}And Canaan begot Zidon his first-born, and Heth; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=16}}and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=17}}and the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=18}}and the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite; and afterward were the families of the Canaanite spread abroad. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=19}}And the border of the Canaanite was from Zidon, as thou goest toward Gerar, unto Gaza; as thou goest toward Sodom and Gomorrah and Admah and Zeboiim, unto Lasha. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=20}}These are the sons of Ham, after their families, after their tongues, in their lands, in their nations.

{{verse|chapter=10|verse=21}}And unto Shem, the father of all the children of Eber, the elder brother of Japheth, to him also were children born. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=22}}The sons of Shem: Elam, and Asshur, and Arpachshad, and Lud, and Aram. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=23}}And the sons of Aram: Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Mash. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=24}}And Arpachshad begot Shelah; and Shelah begot Eber. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=25}}And unto Eber were born two sons; the name of the one was <ref>That is, ''Division.''</ref>Peleg; for in his days was the earth divided; and his brother's name was Joktan. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=26}}And Joktan begot Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=27}}and Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=28}}and Obal, and Abimael, and Sheba; {{verse|chapter=10|verse=29}}and Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab; all these were the sons of Joktan. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=30}}And their dwelling was from Mesha, as thou goest toward Sephar, unto the mountain of the east. {{verse|chapter=10|verse=31}}These are the sons of Shem, after their families, after their tongues, in their lands, after their nations.
