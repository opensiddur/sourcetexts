:O {{sc|Lord}}, hear my prayer, give ear to my supplications;
:In Thy faithfulness answer me, and in Thy righteousness.
:{{verse|143|2|align=float}}And enter not into judgment with Thy servant;
:For in Thy sight shall no man living be justified.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|143|3|align=float}}For the enemy hath persecuted my soul;
:He hath crushed my life down to the ground;
:He hath made me to dwell in darkness, as those that have been long dead.
:{{verse|143|4|align=float}}And my spirit fainteth within me;
:My heart within me is appalled.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|143|5|align=float}}I remember the days of old;
:I meditate on all Thy doing;
:I muse on the work of Thy hands.
:{{verse|143|6|align=float}}I spread forth my hands unto Thee;
:My soul [thirsteth] after Thee, as a weary land. Selah
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|143|7|align=float}}Answer me speedily, O {{sc|Lord}},
:My spirit faileth;
:Hide not Thy face from me;
:Lest I become like them that go down into the pit.
:{{verse|143|8|align=float}}Cause me to hear Thy lovingkindness in the morning,
:For in Thee do I trust;
:Cause me to know the way wherein I should walk,
:For unto Thee have I lifted up my soul.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|143|9|align=float}}Deliver me from mine enemies, O {{sc|Lord}};

:{{verse|143|10|align=float}}Teach me to do Thy will,

:Let Thy good spirit
:Lead me in an even land.
:{{verse|143|11|align=float}}For Thy name's sake, O {{sc|Lord}}, quicken me;
:In Thy righteousness bring my soul out of trouble.
:{{verse|143|12|align=float}}And in Thy mercy cut off mine enemies,

:For I am Thy servant.
