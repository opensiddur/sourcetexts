{{verse|12|9}}And besides that Koheleth was wise, he also taught the people knowledge; yea, he pondered, and sought out, and set in order many proverbs. {{verse|12|10}}Koheleth sought to find out words of delight, and that which was written uprightly, even words of truth.

{{verse|12|11}}The words of the wise are as goads, and as nails well fastened are those that are composed in collections; they are given from one shepherd. {{verse|12|12}}And furthermore, my son, be admonished: of making many books there is no end; and much study is a weariness of the flesh.
