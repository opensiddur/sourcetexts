{{x-larger|FIRST CHRONICLES}}}}


{{dropinitial|{{anchor|1:1}}1}} {{sc|Adam}}, Seth, Enosh; {{verse|1|2}}Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared; {{verse|1|3}}Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech; {{verse|1|4}}Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

{{verse|1|5}}The sons of Japheth: Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras. {{verse|1|6}}And the sons of Gomer: Ashkenaz, and Diphath, and Togarmah. {{verse|1|7}}And the sons of Javan: Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Rodanim.

{{verse|1|8}}The sons of Ham: Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan. {{verse|1|9}}And the sons of Cush: Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raama, and Sabteca. And the sons of Raama: Sheba, and Dedan. {{verse|1|10}}And Cush begot Nimrod; he began to be a mighty one in the earth. {{verse|1|11}}And Mizraim begot Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim, {{verse|1|12}}and Pathrusim, and Casluhim—from whence came the Philistines—and Caphtorim. {{verse|1|13}}And Canaan begot Zidon his first-born, and Heth; {{verse|1|14}}and the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite; {{verse|1|15}}and the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite; {{verse|1|16}}and the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

{{verse|1|17}}The Sons of Shem: Elam, and Asshur, and Arpachshad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech. {{verse|1|18}}And Arpachshad begot Shelah, and Shelah begot Eber. {{verse|1|19}}And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; for in his days the earth was divided; and his brother's name was Joktan. {{verse|1|20}}And Joktan begot Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah; {{verse|1|21}}and Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah; {{verse|1|22}}and Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba; {{verse|1|23}}and Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

{{verse|1|24}}Shem, Arpachshad, Shelah; {{verse|1|25}}Eber, Peleg, Reu; {{verse|1|26}}Serug, Nahor, Terah; {{verse|1|27}}Abram—the same is Abraham. {{verse|1|28}}The sons of Abraham: Isaac, and Ishmael.

{{verse|1|29}}These are their generations: the first-born of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam, {{verse|1|30}}Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema, {{verse|1|31}}Jetur, Naphish, and Kedem. These are the sons of Ishmael.

{{verse|1|32}}And the sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bore Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan: Sheba, and Dedan. {{verse|1|33}}And the sons of Midian: Ephah, and Epher, and Hanoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these were the sons of Keturah.

{{verse|1|34}}And Abraham begot Isaac. The sons of Isaac: Esau, and Israel.

{{verse|1|35}}The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jalam and Korah. {{verse|1|36}}The sons of Eliphaz: Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek. {{verse|1|37}}The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.

{{verse|1|38}}And the sons of Seir: Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan. {{verse|1|39}}And the sons of Lotan: Hori, and Homam;<noinclude>{{smallrefs}}
