{{verse|chapter=17|verse=16}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=17|verse=17}}'Speak unto the children of Israel, and take of them rods, one for each fathers' house, of all their princes according to their fathers' houses, twelve rods; thou shalt write every man's name upon his rod. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=18}}And thou shalt write Aaron's name upon the rod of Levi, for there shall be one rod for the head of their fathers'  houses. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=19}}And thou shalt lay them up in the tent of meeting before the testimony, where I meet with you. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=20}}And it shall come to pass, that the man whom I shall choose, his rod shall bud; and I will make to cease from Me the murmurings of the children of Israel, which they murmur against you.' {{verse|chapter=17|verse=21}}And Moses spoke unto the children of Israel; and all their princes gave him rods, for each prince one, according to their fathers' houses, even twelve rods; and the rod of Aaron was among their rods. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=22}}And Moses laid up the rods before the {{sc|Lord}} in the tent of the testimony. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=23}}And it came to pass on the morrow, that Moses went into the tent of the testimony; and, behold, the rod of Aaron for the house of Levi was budded, and put forth buds, and bloomed blossoms, and bore ripe almonds. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=24}}And Moses brought out all the rods from before the {{sc|Lord}} unto all the children of Israel; and they looked, and took every man his rod.

{{verse|chapter=17|verse=25}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto Moses: 'Put back the rod of Aaron before the testimony, to be kept there, for a token against the rebellious children; that there may be made an end of their murmurings against Me, that they die not.' {{verse|chapter=17|verse=26}}Thus did Moses; as the {{sc|Lord}} commanded him, so did he.

{{verse|chapter=17|verse=27}}And the children of Israel spoke unto Moses, saying: 'Behold, we perish, we are undone, we are all undone. {{verse|chapter=17|verse=28}}Every one that cometh near, that cometh near unto the tabernacle of the {{sc|Lord}}, is to die; shall we wholly perish?'
