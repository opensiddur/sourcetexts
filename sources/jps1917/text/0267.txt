{{verse|27|9}}And Moses and the priests the Levites spoke unto all Israel, saying: 'Keep silence, and hear, O Israel; this day thou art become a people unto the {{sc|Lord}} thy God. {{verse|27|10}}Thou shalt therefore hearken to the voice of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God, and do His commandments and His statutes, which I command thee this day.' {{verse|27|11}}And Moses charged the people the same day, saying: {{verse|27|12}}'These shall stand upon mount Gerizim to bless the people, when ye are passed over the Jordan: Simeon, and Levi, and Judah, and Issachar, and Joseph, and Benjamin; {{verse|27|13}}and these shall stand upon mount Ebal for the curse: Reuben, Gad, and Asher, and Zebulun, Dan, and Naphtali. {{verse|27|14}}And the Levites shall speak, and say unto all the men of Israel with a loud voice:

{{verse|27|15}}Cursed be the man that maketh a graven or molten image, an abomination unto the {{sc|Lord}}, the work of the hands of the craftsman, and setteth it up in secret. And all the people shall answer and say: Amen.

{{verse|27|16}}Cursed be he that dishonoureth his father or his mother. And all the people shall say: Amen.

{{verse|27|17}}Cursed be he that removeth his neighbour's landmark. And all the people shall say: Amen.

{{verse|27|18}}Cursed be he that maketh the blind to go astray in the way. And all the people shall say: Amen.

{{verse|27|19}}Cursed be he that perverteth the justice due to the stranger, fatherless, and widow. And all the people shall say: Amen.

{{verse|27|20}}Cursed be he that lieth with his father's wife; because he hath uncovered his father's skirt. And all the people shall say: Amen. {{verse|27|21}}Cursed be he that lieth with any manner of beast. And all the people shall say: Amen.

{{verse|27|22}}Cursed be he that lieth with his sister, the daughter of his father, or the daughter of his mother. And all the people shall say: Amen.
