{{verse|3|23}}Then said the king: 'The one saith: This is my son that liveth, and thy son is the dead; and the other saith: Nay; but thy son is the dead, and my son is the living.' {{verse|3|24}}And the king said: 'Fetch me a sword.' And they brought a sword before the king. {{verse|3|25}}And the king said: 'Divide the living child in two, and give half to the one, and half to the other.' {{verse|3|26}}Then spoke the woman whose the living child was unto the king, for her heart yearned upon her son, and she said: 'Oh, my lord, give her the living child, and in no wise slay it.' But the other said: 'It shall be neither mine nor thine; divide it.' {{verse|3|27}}Then the king answered and said: 'Give her the living child, and in no wise slay it: she is the mother thereof.' {{verse|3|28}}And all Israel heard of the judgment which the king had judged; and they feared the king; for they saw that the wisdom of God was in him, to do justice.

{{dropinitial|{{anchor|4:1}}4}}And king Solomon was king over all Israel. {{verse|4|2}}And these were the princes whom he had: Azariah the son of Zadok, the priest; {{verse|4|3}}Elihoreph and Ahijah, the sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud, the recorder; {{verse|4|4}}and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the host; and Zadok and Abiathar were priests; {{verse|4|5}}and Azariah the son of Nathan was over the officers; and Zabud the son of Nathan was chief minister and the king's friend; {{verse|4|6}}and Ahishar was over the household; and Adoniram the son of Abda was over the levy.

{{verse|4|7}}And Solomon had twelve officers over all Israel, who provided victuals for the king and his household: each man had to make provision for a month in the year. {{verse|4|8}}And these are their names: The son of Hur, in the hill-country of Ephraim; {{verse|4|9}}the son of Deker, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Beth-shemesh, and Elon-beth-hanan; {{verse|4|10}}the son of Hesed, in Arubboth; to him pertained Socoh, and all the land of Hepher; {{verse|4|11}}the son of Abinadab, in all the region of Dor; he had Taphath the daughter of Solomon to wife; {{verse|4|12}}Baana the son of Ahilud, in Taanach and Megiddo, and all Beth-shean which is beside Zarethan, beneath Jezreel, from Beth-shean to Abel-meholah, as far as beyond Jokmeam; {{verse|4|13}}the son of Geber, in Ramoth-gilead; to him pertained the villages of Jair the son of Manasseh, which are in Gilead; even to him pertained the region of Argob, which is in Bashan, threescore great cities with walls and brazen bars; {{verse|4|14}}Ahinadab the son of Iddo, in Mahanaim; {{verse|4|15}}Ahimaaz, in Naphtali; he also took Basemath the daughter of Solomon to wife; {{verse|4|16}}Baana the son of Hushai, in Asher and Bealoth; {{verse|4|17}}Jehoshaphat the son of Paruah, in Issachar; {{verse|4|18}}Shimei the son of Ela, in Benjamin; {{verse|4|19}}Geber the son of Uri, in the land of Gilead, the country of Sihon king of the Amorites and of Og king of Bashan; and one officer that was [over all the officers] in the land. {{verse|4|20}}Judah and Israel were many, as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, eating and drinking and making merry.
