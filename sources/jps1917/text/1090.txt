{{dropinitial|{{anchor|8:1}}8}} And Benjamin begot Bela his first-born, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third; {{verse|8|2}}Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth. {{verse|8|3}}And Bela had sons, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud; {{verse|8|4}}and Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah; {{verse|8|5}}and Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram. {{verse|8|6}}And these are the sons of Ehud—these are the heads of fathers' houses of the inhabitants of Geba, and they were carried captive to Manahath; {{verse|8|7}}and Naaman, and Ahijah, and Gera, were they that carried them captive—and he begot Uzza, and Ahihud. {{verse|8|8}}And Shaharaim begot children in the field of Moab, after he had sent them away, to wit, Hushim and Baara his wives; {{verse|8|9}}he begot of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcam; {{verse|8|10}}and Jeuz, and Sachiah, and Mirmah. These were his sons, heads of fathers' houses. {{verse|8|11}}and of Hushim he begot Abitub, and Elpaal.

{{verse|8|12}}And the sons of Elpaal: Eber, and Misham, and Shemed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof; {{verse|8|13}}and Beriah, and Shema, who were heads of fathers' houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who put to flight the inhabitants of Gath. {{verse|8|14}}And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth; {{verse|8|15}}and Zebadiah, and Arad, and Eder; {{verse|8|16}}and Michael, and Ishpah, and Joha, were the sons of Beriah. {{verse|8|17}}And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hizki, and Heber; {{verse|8|18}}and Ishmerai, and Izliah, and Jobab, were the sons of Elpaal. {{verse|8|19}}And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi; {{verse|8|20}}and Elienai, and Zillethai, and Eliel; {{verse|8|21}}and Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, were the sons of Shimei. {{verse|8|22}}And Ishpan, and Ebed, and Eliel; {{verse|8|23}}and Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan; {{verse|8|24}}and Hananiah, and Elam, and Anthothiah; {{verse|8|25}}and Iphdeiah, and Penuel, were the sons of Shashak. {{verse|8|26}}And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah; {{verse|8|27}}and Jaareshiah, and Elijah, and Zichri, were the sons of Jeroham. {{verse|8|28}}These were heads of fathers'houses throughout their generations, chief men; these dwelt in Jerusalem.

{{verse|8|29}}And in Gibeon there dwelt the father of Gibeon [, <ref>See [[#9:35|ch. ix. 35]].</ref>Jeiel], whose wife's name was Maacah; {{verse|8|30}}and his first-born son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab; {{verse|8|31}}and Gedor, and Ahio, and Zecher. {{verse|8|32}}And Mikloth begot Shimeah. And they also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against their brethren.

{{verse|8|33}}And Ner begot Kish; and Kish begot Saul; and Saul begot Jonathan, and Malchi-shua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal. {{verse|8|34}}And the son of Jonathan was Merib-baal; and Merib-baal begot Micah. {{verse|8|35}}And the sons of Micah; Pithon, and Melech, and Taarea, and Ahaz. {{verse|8|36}}And Ahaz begot Jehoaddah; and Jehoaddah begot Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begot Moza; {{verse|8|37}}and Moza begot Binea; Raphah was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son. {{verse|8|38}}And Azel had six sons, whose names are these: Azrikam, Bocru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel. {{verse|8|39}}And the sons of Eshek his brother: Ulam his first-born, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third. {{verse|8|40}}And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers; and had many sons, and sons' sons, a hundred and fifty. All these were of the sons of Benjamin.
