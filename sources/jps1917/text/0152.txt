{{verse|chapter=11|verse=9}}These may ye eat of all that are in the waters: whatsoever hath fins and scales in the waters, in the seas, and in the rivers, them may ye eat. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=10}}And all that have not fins and scales in the seas, and in the rivers, of all that swarm in the waters, and of all the living creatures that are in the waters, they are a detestable thing unto you, {{verse|chapter=11|verse=11}}and they shall be a detestable thing unto you; ye shall not eat of their flesh, and their carcasses ye shall have in detestation. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=12}}Whatsoever hath no fins nor scales in the waters, that is a detestable thing unto you.

{{verse|chapter=11|verse=13}}And these ye shall have in detestation among the fowls; they shall not be eaten, they are a detestable thing: the great vulture, and the bearded vulture, and the ospray; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=14}}and the kite, and the falcon after its kinds; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=15}}every raven after its kinds; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=16}}and the ostrich, and the night-hawk, and the sea-mew, and the hawk after its kinds; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=17}}and the little owl, and the cormorant, and the great owl; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=18}}and the horned owl, and the pelican, and the carrion-vulture; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=19}}and the stork, and the heron after its kinds, and the hoopoe, and the bat.

{{verse|chapter=11|verse=20}}All winged swarming things that go upon all fours are a detestable thing unto you. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=21}}Yet these may ye eat of all winged swarming things that go upon all fours, which have jointed legs above their feet, wherewith to leap upon the earth; {{verse|chapter=11|verse=22}}even these of them ye may eat: the locust after its kinds, and the bald locust after its kinds, and the cricket after its kinds, and the grasshopper after its kinds. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=23}}But all winged swarming things, which have four feet, are a detestable thing unto you.

{{verse|chapter=11|verse=24}}And by these ye shall become unclean; whosoever toucheth the carcass of them shall be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=25}}And whosoever beareth aught of the carcass of them shall wash his clothes, and be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=26}}Every beast which parteth the hoof, but is not cloven-footed, nor cheweth the cud, is unclean unto you; every one that to toucheth them shall be unclean. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=27}}And whatsoever goeth upon its paws, among all beasts that go on all fours, they are unclean unto you; whoso toucheth their carcass shall be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=28}}And he that beareth the carcass of them shall wash his clothes, and be unclean until the even; they are unclean unto you.
