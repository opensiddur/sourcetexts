{{dropinitial|{{anchor|14:1}}14}}Then came certain of the elders of Israel unto me, and sat before me. {{verse|14|2}}And the word of the {{sc|Lord}} came unto me, saying: {{verse|14|3}}'Son of man, these men have set up their idols in their mind, and put the stumblingblock of their iniquity before their face; should I be inquired of at all by them? {{verse|14|4}}Therefore speak unto them, and say unto them: Thus saith the Lord {{sc|God}}: Every man of the house of Israel that setteth up his idols in his mind, and putteth the stumblingblock of his iniquity before his face, and cometh to the prophet—I the {{sc|Lord}} will answer him that cometh according to the multitude of his idols; {{verse|14|5}}that I may take the house of Israel in their own heart, because they are all turned away from Me through their idols.

{{verse|14|6}}Therefore say unto the house of Israel: Thus saith the Lord {{sc|God}}: Return ye, and turn yourselves from your idols; and turn away your faces from all your abominations. {{verse|14|7}}For every one of the house of Israel, or of the strangers that sojourn in Israel, that separateth himself from Me, and taketh his idols into his heart, and putteth the stumblingblock of his iniquity before his face, and cometh to the prophet, that he inquire for him of Me—I the {{sc|Lord}} will answer him by Myself, {{verse|14|8}}and I will set My face against that man, and will make him a sign and a proverb, and I will cut him off from the midst of My people; and ye shall know that I am the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|14|9}}And when the prophet is enticed and speaketh a word, I the {{sc|Lord}} have enticed that prophet, and I will stretch out My hand upon him, and will destroy him from the midst of My people Israel. {{verse|14|10}}And they shall bear their iniquity; the iniquity of the prophet shall be even as the iniquity of him that inquireth; {{verse|14|11}}that the house of Israel may go no more astray from Me, neither defile themselves any more with all their transgressions; but that they may be My people, and I may be their God, saith the Lord {{sc|God}}.'
