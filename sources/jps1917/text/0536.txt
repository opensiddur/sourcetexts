:{{verse|30|27|align=float}}Behold, the name of the {{sc|Lord}} cometh from far,
:With His anger burning, and in thick uplifting of smoke;
:His lips are full of indignation,
:And His tongue is as a devouring fire;
:{{verse|30|28|align=float}}And His breath is as an overflowing stream,
:That divideth even unto the neck,
:To sift the nations with the sieve of destruction;
:And a bridle that causeth to err shall be in the jaws of the peoples.
:{{verse|30|29|align=float}}Ye shall have a song
:As in the night when a feast is hallowed;
:And gladness of heart, as when one goeth with the pipe
:To come into the mountain of the {{sc|Lord}}, to the Rock of Israel.
:{{verse|30|30|align=float}}And the {{sc|Lord}} will cause His glorious voice to be heard,
:And will show the lighting down of His arm,
:With furious anger, and the flame of a devouring fire,
:With a bursting of clouds, and a storm of rain, and hailstones.
:{{verse|30|31|align=float}}For through the voice of the {{sc|Lord}} shall Asshur be dismayed,
:The rod with which He smote.
:{{verse|30|32|align=float}}And in every place where the appointed staff shall pass,
:Which the {{sc|Lord}} shall lay upon him,
:It shall be with tabrets and harps;
:And in battles of wielding will He fight with them.
:{{verse|30|33|align=float}}For a hearth is ordered of old;
:Yea, for the king it is prepared,
:Deep and large;
:The pile thereof is fire and much wood;
:The breath of the {{sc|Lord}}, like a stream of brimstone, doth kindle it.

:{{dropinitial|{{anchor|31:1}}31}}Woe to them that go down to Egypt for help,
:And rely on horses,
:And trust in chariots, because they are many,
:And in horsemen, because they are exceeding mighty;
:But they look not unto the Holy One of Israel,
:Neither seek the {{sc|Lord}}!
:{{verse|31|2|align=float}}Yet He also is wise,
:And bringeth evil,
:And doth not call back His words;
:But will arise against the house of the evil-doers,
:And against the help of them that work iniquity.
:{{verse|31|3|align=float}}Now the Egyptians are men, and not God,
:And their horses flesh, and not spirit;
:So when the {{sc|Lord}} shall stretch out His hand,
:Both he that helpeth shall stumble, and he that is helped shall fall,
:And they all shall perish together.

:Nor abase himself for the noise of them;
:So will the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts come down
:To fight upon mount Zion, and upon the hill thereof.
