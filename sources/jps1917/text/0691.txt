{{dropinitial|{{anchor|22:1}}22}}Moreover the word of the {{sc|Lord}} came unto me, saying: {{verse|22|2}}'Now, thou, son of man, wilt thou judge, wilt thou judge the bloody city? then cause her to know all her abominations. {{verse|22|3}}And thou shalt say: Thus saith the Lord {{sc|God}}: O city that sheddest blood in the midst of thee, that thy time may come, and that makest idols unto thyself to defile thee; {{verse|22|4}}thou art become guilty in thy blood that thou hast shed, and art defiled in thine idols which thou hast made; and thou hast caused thy days to draw near, and art come even unto thy years; therefore have I made thee a reproach unto the nations, and a mocking to all the countries! {{verse|22|5}}Those that are near, and those that are far from thee, shall mock thee, thou defiled of name and full of tumult.

{{verse|22|6}}Behold, the princes of Israel, every one according to his might, have been in thee to shed blood. {{verse|22|7}}In thee have they made light of father and mother; in the midst of thee have they dealt by oppression with the stranger; in thee have they wronged the fatherless and the widow. {{verse|22|8}}Thou hast despised My holy things, and hast profaned My sabbaths. {{verse|22|9}}In thee have been talebearers to shed blood; and in thee they have eaten upon the mountains; in the midst of thee they have committed lewdness. {{verse|22|10}}In thee have they uncovered their fathers' nakedness; in thee have they humbled her that was unclean in her impurity. {{verse|22|11}}And each hath committed abomination with his neighbour's wife; and each hath lewdly defiled his daughter-in-law; and each in thee hath humbled his sister, his father's daughter. {{verse|22|12}}In thee have they taken gifts to shed blood; thou hast taken interest and increase, and thou hast greedily gained of thy neighbours by oppression, and hast forgotten Me, saith the Lord {{sc|God}}.
