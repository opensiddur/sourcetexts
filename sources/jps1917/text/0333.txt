{{verse|12|8}}And after him Ibzan of Beth-lehem judged Israel. {{verse|12|9}}And he had thirty sons, and thirty daughters he sent abroad, and thirty daughters he brought in from abroad for his sons. And he judged Israel seven years. {{verse|12|10}}and Ibzan died, and was buried at Beth-lehem. 

{{verse|12|11}}And after him Elon the Zebulunite judged Israel; and he judged Israel ten years. {{verse|12|12}}And Elon the Zebulunite died, and was buried in Aijalon in the land of Zebulun.

{{verse|12|13}}And after him Abdon the son of Hillel the Pirathonite judged Israel. {{verse|12|14}}And he had forty sons and thirty sons' sons, that rode on threescore and ten ass colts; and he judged Israel eight years. {{verse|12|15}}And Abdon the son of Hillel the Pirathonite died, and was buried in Pirathon in the land of Ephraim, in the hill-country of the Amalekites.
