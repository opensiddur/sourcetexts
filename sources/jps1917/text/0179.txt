{{dropinitial|{{anchor|1:1}}1}}{{sc|And}} the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, in the tent of meeting, on the first day of the second month, in the second year after they were come out of the land of Egypt, saying: {{verse|chapter=1|verse=2}}'Take ye the sum of all the congregation of the children of Israel, by their families, by their fathers' houses, according to the number of names, every male, by their polls; {{verse|chapter=1|verse=3}}from twenty years old and upward, all that are able to go forth to war in Israel: ye shall number them by their hosts, even thou and Aaron. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=4}}And with you there shall be a man of every tribe, every one head of his {{SIC|fathers'|father's}} house. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=5}}And these are the names of the men that shall stand with you: of Reuben, Elizur the son of Shedeur. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=6}}Of Simeon, Shelumiel the son of Zurishaddai. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=7}}Of Judah, Nahshon the son of Amminadab. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=8}}Of Issachar, Nethanel the son of Zuar. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=9}}Of Zebulun, Eliab the son of Helon. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=10}}Of the children of Joseph: of Ephraim, Elishama the son of Ammihud; of Manasseh, Gamaliel the son of Pedahzur. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=11}}Of Benjamin, Abidan the son of Gideoni. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=12}}Of Dan, Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=13}}Of Asher, Pagiel the son of Ochran. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=14}}Of Gad, Eliasaph the son of Deuel. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=15}}Of Naphtali, Ahira the son of Enan.' {{verse|chapter=1|verse=16}}These were the elect of the congregation, the princes of the tribes of their fathers; they were the heads of the thousands of Israel. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=17}}And Moses and Aaron took these men that are pointed out by name. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=18}}And they assembled all the congregation together on the first day of the second month, and they declared their pedigrees after their families, by their fathers' houses, according to the number of names, from twenty years old and upward, by their polls. {{verse|chapter=1|verse=19}}As the {{sc|Lord}} commanded Moses, so did he number them in the wilderness of Sinai.

{{verse|chapter=1|verse=20}}And the children of Reuben, Israel's first-born, their generations, by their families, by their fathers' houses, according to the number of names, by their polls, every male from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war; {{verse|chapter=1|verse=21}}those that were numbered of them, of the tribe of Reuben, were forty and six thousand and five hundred.

{{verse|chapter=1|verse=22}}Of the children of Simeon, their generations, by their families, by their fathers' houses, those that were numbered thereof, according to the number of names, by their polls, every male from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war; {{verse|chapter=1|verse=23}}those that were numbered of them, of the tribe of Simeon, were fifty and nine thousand and three hundred.

{{verse|chapter=1|verse=24}}Of the children of Gad, their generations, by their families, by their fathers' houses, according to the number of names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war; {{verse|chapter=1|verse=25}}those that were numbered of them, of the tribe of Gad, were forty and five thousand six hundred and fifty.
