{{verse|12|20}}When the {{sc|Lord}} thy God shall enlarge thy border, as He hath promised thee, and thou shalt say: 'I will eat flesh', because thy soul desireth to eat flesh; thou mayest eat flesh, after all the desire of thy soul. {{verse|12|21}}If the place which the {{sc|Lord}} thy God shall choose to put His name there be too far from thee, then thou shalt kill of thy herd and of thy flock, which the {{sc|Lord}} hath given thee, as I have commanded thee, and thou shalt eat within thy gates, after all the desire of thy soul. {{verse|12|22}}Howbeit as the gazelle and as the hart is eaten, so thou shalt eat thereof; the unclean and the clean may eat thereof alike. {{verse|12|23}}Only be stedfast in not eating the blood; for the blood is the life; and thou shalt not eat the life with the flesh. {{verse|12|24}}Thou shalt not eat it; thou shalt pour it out upon the earth as water. {{verse|12|25}}Thou shalt not eat it; that it may go well with thee, and with thy children after thee, when thou shalt do that which is right in the eyes of the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|12|26}}Only thy holy things which thou hast, and thy vows, thou shalt take, and go unto the place which the {{sc|Lord}} shall choose; {{verse|12|27}}and thou shalt offer thy burnt-offerings, the flesh and the blood, upon the altar of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God; and the blood of thy sacrifices shall be poured out against the altar of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God, and thou shalt eat the flesh. {{verse|12|28}}Observe and hear all these words which I command thee, that it may go well with thee, and with thy children after thee for ever, when thou doest that which is good and right in the eyes of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God.

{{verse|12|29}}When the {{sc|Lord}} thy God shall cut off the nations from before thee, whither thou goest in to dispossess them, and thou dispossessest them, and dwellest in their land; {{verse|12|30}}take heed to thyself that thou be not ensnared to follow them, after that they are destroyed from before thee; and that thou inquire not after their gods, saying: 'How used these nations to serve their gods? even so will I do likewise.' {{verse|12|31}}Thou shalt not do so unto the {{sc|Lord}} thy God; for every abomination to the {{sc|Lord}}, which He hateth, have they done unto their gods; for even their sons and their daughters do they burn in the fire to their gods.
