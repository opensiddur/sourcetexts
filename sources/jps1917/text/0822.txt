{{dropinitial|{{anchor|36:1}}36}} For the Leader. [A Psalm] of David the servant of the {{sc|Lord}}


:{{verse|36|2|align=float}}Transgression speaketh to the wicked, methinks—
:There is no fear of God before his eyes.
:{{verse|36|3|align=float}}For it flattereth him in his eyes,
:Until his iniquity be found, and he be hated.
:{{verse|36|4|align=float}}The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit;
:He hath left off to be wise, to do good.
:{{verse|36|5|align=float}}He deviseth iniquity upon his bed;
:He setteth himself in a way that is not good;
:He abhorreth not evil.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|36|6|align=float}}Thy lovingkindness, O {{sc|Lord}}, is in the heavens;
:Thy faithfulness reacheth unto the skies.
:{{verse|36|7|align=float}}Thy righteousness is like the mighty mountains;
:Thy judgments are like the great deep;
:Man and beast Thou preservest, O {{sc|Lord}}.
:{{verse|36|8|align=float}}How precious is Thy lovingkindness, O God!
:And the children of men take refuge in the shadow of Thy wings.
:{{verse|36|9|align=float}}They are abundantly satisfied with the fatness of Thy house;
:And Thou makest them drink of the river of Thy pleasures.
:{{verse|36|10|align=float}}For with Thee is the fountain of life;
:In Thy light do we see light.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|36|11|align=float}}O continue Thy lovingkindness unto them that know Thee;
:And Thy righteousness to the upright in heart.
:{{verse|36|12|align=float}}Let not the foot of pride overtake me,
:And let not the hand of the wicked drive me away.
:{{verse|36|13|align=float}}There are the workers of iniquity fallen;
:They are thrust down, and are not able to rise.
