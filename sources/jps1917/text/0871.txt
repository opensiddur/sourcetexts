{{dropinitial|{{anchor|103:1}}103}} [A Psalm] of David.


:Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, O my soul;
:And all that is within me, bless His holy name.
:{{verse|103|2|align=float}}Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, O my soul,
:And forget not all His benefits;
:{{verse|103|3|align=float}}Who forgiveth all thine iniquity;
:Who healeth all Thy diseases;
:{{verse|103|4|align=float}}Who redeemeth Thy life from the pit;
:Who encompasseth thee with lovingkindness and tender mercies;
:{{verse|103|5|align=float}}Who satisfieth thine old age with good things;
:So that Thy youth is renewed like the eagle.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|103|6|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} executeth righteousness,
:And acts of justice for all that are oppressed.
:{{verse|103|7|align=float}}He made known His ways unto Moses,
:His doings unto the children of Israel.
:{{verse|103|8|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} is full of compassion and gracious,
:Slow to anger, and plenteous in mercy.
:{{verse|103|9|align=float}}He will not always contend;
:Neither will He keep His anger for ever.
:{{verse|103|10|align=float}}He hath not dealt with us after our sins,
:Nor requited us according to our iniquities.
:{{verse|103|11|align=float}}For as the heaven is high above the earth,
:So great is His mercy toward them that fear Him.
:{{verse|103|12|align=float}}As far as the east is from the west,
:So far hath He removed our transgressions from us.
:{{verse|103|13|align=float}}Like as a father hath compassion upon his children,
:So hath the {{sc|Lord}} compassion upon them that fear Him. {{verse|103|14|align=float}}For He knoweth our frame;
:He remembereth that we are dust.
:{{verse|103|15|align=float}}As for man, his days are as grass;
:As a flower of the field, so he flourisheth.
:{{verse|103|16|align=float}}For the wind passeth over it, and it is gone;
:And the place thereof knoweth it no more.
:{{verse|103|17|align=float}}But the mercy of the {{sc|Lord}} is from everlasting to everlasting upon them that fear Him,
:And His righteousness unto children's children;
:{{verse|103|18|align=float}}To such as keep His covenant,
:And to those that remember His precepts to do them.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|103|19|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} hath established His throne in the heavens;
:And His kingdom ruleth over all.
:{{verse|103|20|align=float}}Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, ye angels of His,
:Ye mighty in strength, that fulfil His word,
:Hearkening unto the voice of His word.
:{{verse|103|21|align=float}}Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, all ye His hosts;
:Ye ministers of His, that do His pleasure.
:{{verse|103|22|align=float}}Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, all ye His works,
:In all places of His dominion;
:Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, O my soul.
