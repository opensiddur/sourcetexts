And as for the flesh, every one that is clean may eat thereof. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=20}}But the soul that eateth of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace-offerings, that pertain unto the {{sc|Lord}}, having his uncleanness upon him, that soul shall be cut off from his people. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=21}}And when any one shall touch any unclean thing, whether it be the uncleanness of man, or an unclean beast, or any unclean detestable thing, and eat of the flesh of the sacrifice of peace-offerings, which pertain unto the {{sc|Lord}}, that soul shall be cut off from his people.

{{verse|chapter=7|verse=22}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=7|verse=23}}Speak unto the children of Israel, saying:

Ye shall eat no fat, of ox, or sheep, or goat. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=24}}And the fat of that which dieth of itself, and the fat of that which is torn of beasts, may be used for any other service; but ye shall in no wise eat of it. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=25}}For whosoever eateth the fat of the beast, of which men present an offering made by fire unto the {{sc|Lord}}, even the soul that eateth it shall be cut off from his people. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=26}}And ye shall eat no manner of blood, whether it be of fowl or of beast, in any of your dwellings. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=27}}Whosoever it be that eateth any blood, that soul shall be cut off from his people.

{{verse|chapter=7|verse=28}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=7|verse=29}}Speak unto the children of Israel, saying:

He that offereth his sacrifice of peace-offerings unto the {{sc|Lord}} shall bring his offering unto the {{sc|Lord}} out of his sacrifice of peace-offerings. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=30}}His own hands shall bring the offerings of the {{sc|Lord}} made by fire: the fat with the breast shall he bring, that the breast may be waved for a wave-offering before the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=31}}And the priest shall make the fat smoke upon the altar; but the breast shall be Aaron's and his sons'. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=32}}And the right thigh shall ye give unto the priest for a heave-offering out of your sacrifices of peace-offerings. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=33}}He among the sons of Aaron, that offereth the blood of the peace-offerings, and the fat, shall have the right thigh for a portion. {{verse|chapter=7|verse=34}}For the breast of waving and the thigh of heaving have I taken of the children of Israel out of their sacrifices of peace-offerings, and have given them unto Aaron the priest and unto his sons as a due for ever from the children of Israel.
