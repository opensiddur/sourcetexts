{{verse|3|17}}And the sons of Jeconiah—the same is Assir—Shealtiel his son; {{verse|3|18}}and Malchiram, and Pedaiah, and Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah. {{verse|3|19}}And the sons of Pedaiah: Zerubbabel, and Shimei.
