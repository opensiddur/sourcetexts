<noinclude><pagequality level="4" user="Kathleen.wright5" />{{rh|{{larger|41}}.45|GENESIS|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>Egypt.—{{verse|chapter=41|verse=46}}And Joseph was thirty years old when he stood before Pharaoh king of Egypt.—And Joseph went out from the presence of Pharaoh, and went throughout all the land of Egypt. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=47}}And in the seven years of plenty the earth brought forth in heaps. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=48}}And he gathered up all the food of the seven years which were in the land of Egypt, and laid up the food in the cities; the food of the field, which was round about every city, laid he up in the same. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=49}}And Joseph laid up corn as the sand of the sea, very much, until they left off numbering; for it was without number. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=50}}And unto Joseph were born two sons before the year of famine came, whom Asenath the daughter of Poti-phera priest of On bore unto him. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=51}}And Joseph called the name of the first-born <ref>That is, ''Making to forget.''</ref>Manasseh: 'for God hath made me forget all my toil, and all my father's house.' {{verse|chapter=41|verse=52}}And the name of the second called he <ref>From a Hebrew word signifying ''to be fruitful.''</ref>Ephraim: 'for God hath made me fruitful in the land of my affliction.' {{verse|chapter=41|verse=53}}And the seven years of plenty, that was in the land of Egypt, came to an end. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=54}}And the seven years of famine began to come, according as Joseph had said; and there was famine in all lands; but in all the land of Egypt there was bread. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=55}}And when all the land of Egypt was famished, the people cried to Pharaoh for bread; and Pharaoh said unto all the Egyptians: 'Go unto Joseph; what he saith to you, do.' {{verse|chapter=41|verse=56}}And the famine was over all the face of the earth; and Joseph opened all the store-houses, and sold unto the Egyptians; and the famine was sore in the land of Egypt. {{verse|chapter=41|verse=57}}And all countries came into Egypt to Joseph to buy corn; because the famine was sore in all the earth.
