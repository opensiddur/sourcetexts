<noinclude><pagequality level="4" user="Kathleen.wright5" />{{rh|{{larger|50}}.15|GENESIS|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>Joseph's brethren saw that their father was dead, they said: 'It may be that Joseph will hate us, and will fully requite us all the evil which we did unto him.' {{verse|chapter=50|verse=16}}And they sent a message unto Joseph, saying: 'Thy father did command before he died, saying: {{verse|chapter=50|verse=17}}So shall ye say unto Joseph: Forgive, I pray thee now, the transgression of thy brethren, and their sin, for that they did unto thee evil. And now, we pray thee, forgive the transgression of the servants of the God of thy father.' And Joseph wept when they spoke unto him. {{verse|chapter=50|verse=18}}And his brethren also went and fell down before his face; and they said: 'Behold, we are thy bondmen.' {{verse|chapter=50|verse=19}}And Joseph said unto them: 'Fear not; for am I in the place of God? {{verse|chapter=50|verse=20}}And as for you, ye meant evil against me; but God meant it for good, to bring to pass, as it is this day, to save much people alive. {{verse|chapter=50|verse=21}}Now therefore fear ye not; I will sustain you, and your little ones.' And he comforted them, and spoke kindly unto them.

{{verse|chapter=50|verse=22}}And Joseph dwelt in Egypt, he, and his father's house; and Joseph lived a hundred and ten years. {{verse|chapter=50|verse=23}}And Joseph saw Ephraim's children of the third generation; the children also of Machir the son of Manasseh were born upon Joseph's knees. {{verse|chapter=50|verse=24}}And Joseph said unto his brethren: 'I die; but God will surely remember you, and bring you up out of this land unto the land which He swore to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob.' {{verse|chapter=50|verse=25}}And Joseph took an oath of the children of Israel, saying: 'God will surely remember you, and ye shall carry up my bones from hence.' {{verse|chapter=50|verse=26}}So Joseph died, being a hundred and ten years old. And they embalmed him, and he was put in a coffin in Egypt.<noinclude>{{c|64}}</noinclude>
