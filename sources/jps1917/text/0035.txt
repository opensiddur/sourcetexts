{{dropinitial|{{anchor|11:1}}11}}And the whole earth was of one language and of one speech. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=2}}And it came to pass, as they journeyed east, that they found a plain in the land of Shinar; and they dwelt there. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=3}}And they said one to another: 'Come, let us make brick, and burn them thoroughly.' And they had brick for stone, and slime had they for mortar. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=4}}And they said: 'Come, let us build us a city, and a tower, with its top in heaven, and let us make us a name; lest we be scattered abroad upon the face of the whole earth.' {{verse|chapter=11|verse=5}}And the {{sc|Lord}} came down to see the city and the tower, which the children of men builded. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=6}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said: 'Behold, they are one people, and they have all one language; and this is what they begin to do; and now nothing will be withholden from them, which they purpose to do. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=7}}Come, let us go down, and there confound their language, that they may not understand one another's speech.' {{verse|chapter=11|verse=8}}So the {{sc|Lord}} scattered them abroad from thence upon the face of all the earth; and they left off to build the city. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=9}}Therefore was the name of it called Babel; because the {{sc|Lord}} did there <ref>Heb. ''balal'', to confound.</ref>confound the language of all the earth; and from thence did the {{sc|Lord}} scatter them abroad upon the face of all the earth.
