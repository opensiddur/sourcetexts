{{dropinitial|{{anchor|1:1}}1}}{{sc|The}} Vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem, in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah.


:{{verse|1|2|align=float}}Hear, O heavens, and give ear, O earth,
:For the {{sc|Lord}} hath spoken:
:Children I have reared, and brought up,
:And they have rebelled against Me.
:{{verse|1|3|align=float}}The ox knoweth his owner,
:And the ass his master's crib;
:But Israel doth not know,
:My people doth not consider.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|1|4|align=float}}Ah sinful nation,
:A people laden with iniquity,
:A seed of evil-doers,
:Children that deal corruptly;
:They have forsaken the {{sc|Lord}},
:They have contemned the Holy One of Israel,
:They are turned away backward.
:{{verse|1|5|align=float}}On what part will ye yet be stricken,
:Seeing ye stray away more and more?
:The whole head is sick,
:And the whole heart faint;
:{{verse|1|6|align=float}}From the sole of the foot even unto the head
:There is no soundness in it;
:But wounds, and bruises, and festering sores:
:They have not been pressed, neither bound up,
:Neither mollified with oil.
:{{verse|1|7|align=float}}Your country is desolate;
:Your cities are burned with fire;
:Your land, strangers devour it in your presence,
:And it is desolate, as overthrown by floods.
:{{verse|1|8|align=float}}And the daughter of Zion is left
:As a booth in a vineyard,
:As a lodge in a garden of cucumbers,
:As a besieged city.
:{{verse|1|9|align=float}}Except the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts
:Had left unto us a very small remnant,
:We should have been as Sodom,
:We should have been like unto Gomorrah.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|1|10|align=float}}Hear the word of the {{sc|Lord}},
:Ye rulers of Sodom;
:Give ear unto the law of our God,
:Ye people of Gomorrah.
:{{verse|1|11|align=float}}To what purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto Me?
:Saith the {{sc|Lord}};
:I am full of the burnt-offerings of rams,
:And the fat of fed beasts; And I delight not in the blood
:Of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he-goats.
:{{verse|1|12|align=float}}When ye come to appear before Me,
:Who hath required this at your hand,
:To trample My courts?
