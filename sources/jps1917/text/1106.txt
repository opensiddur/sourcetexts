<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|22}}.7|FIRST CHRONICLES|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{hwe|vid|David}} said to Solomon: 'My son, as for me, it was in my heart to build a house unto the name of the {{sc|Lord}} my God. {{verse|22|8}}But the word of the {{sc|Lord}} came to me, saying: Thou hast shed blood abundantly, and hast made great wars; thou shalt not build a house unto My name, because thou hast shed much blood upon the earth in My sight. {{verse|22|9}}Behold, a son shall be born to thee, who shall be a man of rest; and I will give him rest from all his enemies round about; for his name shall be <ref>That is, ''Peaceful.''</ref>Solomon, and I will give peace and quietness unto Israel in his days. {{verse|22|10}}He shall build a house for My name; and he shall be to Me for a son, and I will be to him for a father; and I will establish the throne of his kingdom over Israel for ever. {{verse|22|11}}Now, my son, the {{sc|Lord}} be with thee; and prosper thou, and build the house of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God, as He hath spoken concerning thee. {{verse|22|12}}Only the {{sc|Lord}} give thee discretion and understanding, and give thee charge concerning Israel; that so thou mayest keep the law of the {{sc|Lord}} thy God. {{verse|22|13}}Then shalt thou prosper, if thou observe to do the statutes and the ordinances which the {{sc|Lord}} charged Moses with concerning Israel; be strong, and of good courage; fear not, neither be dismayed. {{verse|22|14}}Now, behold, in my straits I have prepared for the house of the {{sc|Lord}} a hundred thousand talents of gold, and a thousand thousand talents of silver; and of brass and iron without weight, for it is in abundance; timber also and stone have I prepared; and thou mayest add thereto. {{verse|22|15}}Moreover there are workmen with thee in abundance, hewers and workers of stone and timber, and all men that are skilful in any manner of work; {{verse|22|16}}of the gold, the silver, and the brass, and the iron, there is no number. Arise and be doing, and the {{sc|Lord}} be with thee.'

{{verse|22|17}}David also commanded all the princes of Israel to help Solomon his son: {{verse|22|18}}'Is not the {{sc|Lord}} your God with you? and hath He not given you rest on every side? for He hath delivered the inhabitants of the land into my hand; and the land is subdued before the {{sc|Lord}}, and before His people. {{verse|22|19}}Now set your heart and your soul to seek after the {{sc|Lord}} your God; arise therefore, and build ye the sanctuary of the {{sc|Lord}} God, to bring the ark of the covenant of the {{sc|Lord}}, and the holy vessels of God, into the house that is to be built to the name of the {{sc|Lord}}.'
