:A dwelling-place for jackals,
:An astonishment, and a hissing,

:They shall growl as lions' whelps.
:{{verse|51|39|align=float}}With their poison I will prepare their feast,
:And I will make them drunken, that they may be convulsed,
:And sleep a perpetual sleep, and not wake,

:And the praise of the whole earth seized!
:How is Babylon become an astonishment
:Among the nations!
:{{verse|51|42|align=float}}The sea is come up upon Babylon;
:She is covered with the multitude of the waves thereof.
:{{verse|51|43|align=float}}Her cities are become a desolation,
:A dry land, and a desert,
:A land wherein no man dwelleth,
:Neither doth any son of man pass thereby.
:{{verse|51|44|align=float}}And I will punish Bel in Babylon,
:And I will bring forth out of his mouth that which he hath swallowed up,
:And the nations shall not flow any more unto him;
:Yea, the wall of Babylon shall fall.
:{{verse|51|45|align=float}}My people, go ye out of the midst of her,
