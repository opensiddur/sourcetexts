{{dropinitial|{{anchor|19:1}}19}} Then Job answered and said:

:{{verse|19|2|align=float}}How long will ye vex my soul,
:And crush me with words?
:{{verse|19|3|align=float}}These ten times have ye reproached me;
:Ye are not ashamed that ye deal harshly with me.
:{{verse|19|4|align=float}}And be it indeed that I have erred,
:Mine error remaineth with myself.
:{{verse|19|5|align=float}}If indeed ye will magnify yourselves against me,
:And plead against me my reproach;
:{{verse|19|6|align=float}}Know now that God hath subverted my cause,
:And hath compassed me with His net.
:{{verse|19|7|align=float}}Behold, I cry out: 'Violence! ' but I am not heard;
:I cry aloud, but there is no justice.
:{{verse|19|8|align=float}}He hath fenced up my way that I cannot pass,
:And hath set darkness in my paths.
:{{verse|19|9|align=float}}He hath stripped me of my glory,
:And taken the crown from my head.
:{{verse|19|10|align=float}}He hath broken me down on every side, and I am gone;
:And my hope hath He plucked up like a tree.
:{{verse|19|11|align=float}}He hath also kindled His wrath against me,
:And He counteth me unto Him as one of His adversaries.
:{{verse|19|12|align=float}}His troops come on together,
:And cast up their way against me,
:And encamp round about my tent.
:{{verse|19|13|align=float}}He hath put my brethren far from me,
:And mine acquaintance are wholly estranged from me.
:{{verse|19|14|align=float}}My kinsfolk have failed,
:And my familiar friends have forgotten me.
:{{verse|19|15|align=float}}They that dwell in my house, and my maids, count me for a stranger;
:I am become an alien in their sight.
:{{verse|19|16|align=float}}I call unto my servant, and he giveth me no answer,
:Though I entreat him with my mouth.
:{{verse|19|17|align=float}}My breath is abhorred of my wife,
:And I am loathsome to the children of my tribe.
:{{verse|19|18|align=float}}Even urchins despised me;
:If I arise, they speak against me.
:{{verse|19|19|align=float}}All my intimate friends abhor me;
:And they whom I loved are turned against me.
:{{verse|19|20|align=float}}My bone cleaveth to my skin and to my flesh,
:And I am escaped with the skin of my teeth.
