{{dropinitial|{{anchor|69:1}}69}} For the Leader; upon Shoshannim. [A Psalm] of David.


:{{verse|69|2|align=float}}Save me, O God;
:For the waters are come in even unto the soul.
:{{verse|69|3|align=float}}I am sunk in deep mire, where there is no standing;
:I am come into deep waters, and the flood overwhelmeth me.
:{{verse|69|4|align=float}}I am weary of my crying; my throat is dried;
:Mine eyes fail while I wait for my God.
:{{verse|69|5|align=float}}They that hate me without a cause are more than the hairs of my head;
:They that would cut me off, being mine enemies wrongfully, are many;
:Should I restore that which I took not away?
:{{verse|69|6|align=float}}O God, Thou knowest my folly;
:And my trespasses are not hid from Thee.
:{{verse|69|7|align=float}}Let not them that wait for Thee be ashamed through me, O Lord {{sc|God}} of hosts;
:Let not those that seek Thee be brought to confusion through me, O God of Israel.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|69|8|align=float}}Because for Thy sake I have borne reproach;
:Confusion hath covered my face.
:{{verse|69|9|align=float}}I am become a stranger unto my brethren,
:And an alien unto my mother's children.
:{{verse|69|10|align=float}}Because zeal for Thy house hath eaten me up,
:And the reproaches of them that reproach Thee are fallen upon me.
:{{verse|69|11|align=float}}And I wept with my soul with fasting,
:And that became unto me a reproach.
:{{verse|69|12|align=float}}I made sackcloth also my garment,
:And I became a byword unto them.
:{{verse|69|13|align=float}}They that sit in the gate talk of me;
:And I am the song of the drunkards.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|69|14|align=float}}But as for me, let my prayer be unto Thee, O {{sc|Lord}}, in an acceptable time;
:O God, in the abundance of Thy mercy,
:Answer me with the truth of Thy salvation.
:{{verse|69|15|align=float}}Deliver me out of the mire, and let me not sink;
:Let me be delivered from them that hate me, and out of the deep waters.
:{{verse|69|16|align=float}}Let not the waterflood overwhelm me,
:Neither let the deep swallow me up;
:And let not the pit shut her mouth upon me.
:{{verse|69|17|align=float}}Answer me, O {{sc|Lord}}, for Thy mercy is good;
