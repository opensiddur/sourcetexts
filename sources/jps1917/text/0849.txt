{{dropinitial|{{anchor|73:1}}73}}
A Psalm of Asaph.


:Surely God is good to Israel,
:Even to such as are pure in heart.
:{{verse|73|2|align=float}}But as for me, my feet were almost gone;
:My steps had well nigh slipped.
:{{verse|73|3|align=float}}For I was envious at the arrogant,
:When I saw the prosperity of the wicked.
:{{verse|73|4|align=float}}For there are no pangs at their death,
:And their body is sound.
:{{verse|73|5|align=float}}In the trouble of man they are not;
:Neither are they plagued like men.
:{{verse|73|6|align=float}}Therefore pride is as a chain about their neck;
:Violence covereth them as a garment.
:{{verse|73|7|align=float}}Their eyes stand forth from fatness;
:They are gone beyond the imaginations of their heart.
:{{verse|73|8|align=float}}They scoff, and in wickedness utter oppression;
:They speak as if there were none on high.
:{{verse|73|9|align=float}}They have set their mouth against the heavens,
:And their tongue walketh through the earth.
:{{verse|73|10|align=float}}Therefore His people return hither;
:And waters of fullness are drained out by them.
:{{verse|73|11|align=float}}And they say: 'How doth God know?
:And is there knowledge in the Most High?'
:{{verse|73|12|align=float}}Behold, such are the wicked;
:And they that are always at ease increase riches.
:{{verse|73|13|align=float}}Surely in vain have I cleansed my heart,
:And washed my hands in innocency;
:{{verse|73|14|align=float}}For all the day have I been plagued,
:And my chastisement came every morning.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|73|15|align=float}}If I had said: 'I will speak thus',
:Behold, I had been faithless to the generation of Thy children.
:{{verse|73|16|align=float}}And when I pondered how I might know this,
:It was wearisome in mine eyes;
:{{verse|73|17|align=float}}Until I entered into the sanctuary of God,
:And considered their end.
:{{verse|73|18|align=float}}Surely Thou settest them in slippery places;
:Thou hurlest them down to utter ruin.
:{{verse|73|19|align=float}}How are they become a desolation in a moment!
:They are wholly consumed by terrors.
:{{verse|73|20|align=float}}As a dream when one awaketh,
:So, O Lord, when Thou arousest Thyself, Thou wilt despise their semblance.
:{{verse|73|21|align=float}}For my heart was in a ferment,
:And I was pricked in my reins.
:{{verse|73|22|align=float}}But I was brutish, and ignorant;
:I was as a beast before Thee.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|73|23|align=float}}Nevertheless I am continually with Thee;
:Thou holdest my right hand.
:{{verse|73|24|align=float}}Thou wilt guide me with Thy counsel,
:And afterward receive me with glory.
:{{verse|73|25|align=float}}Whom have I in heaven but Thee?
:And beside Thee I desire none upon earth.
:{{verse|73|26|align=float}}My flesh and my heart faileth;
:But God is the rock of my heart and my portion for ever.
:{{verse|73|27|align=float}}For, lo, they that go far from Thee shall perish;
:Thou dost destroy all them that go astray from Thee.<noinclude>{{smallrefs}}
