:{{verse|30|9|align=float}}And now I am become their song,
:Yea, I am a byword unto them.
:{{verse|30|10|align=float}}They abhor me, they flee far from me,
:And spare not to spit in my face.
:{{verse|30|11|align=float}}For He hath loosed my cord, and afflicted me,
:And they have cast off the bridle before me.
:{{verse|30|12|align=float}}Upon my right hand rise the brood;
:They entangle my feet,
:And they cast up against me their ways of destruction.
:{{verse|30|13|align=float}}They break up my path,
:They further my calamity,
:Even men that have no helper.
:{{verse|30|14|align=float}}As through a wide breach they come;
:In the midst of the ruin they roll themselves upon me.
:{{verse|30|15|align=float}}Terrors are turned upon me,

:And my welfare is passed away as a cloud.
