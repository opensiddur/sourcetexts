{{verse|11|40}}Solomon sought therefore to kill Jeroboam; but Jeroboam arose, and fled into Egypt, unto Shishak king of Egypt, and was in Egypt until the death of Solomon.

{{verse|11|41}}Now the rest of the acts of Solomon, and all that he did, and his wisdom, are they not written in the book of the acts of Solomon? {{verse|11|42}}And the time that Solomon reigned in Jerusalem over all Israel was forty years. {{verse|11|43}}And Solomon slept with his fathers, and was buried in the city of David his father; and Rehoboam his son reigned in his stead.
