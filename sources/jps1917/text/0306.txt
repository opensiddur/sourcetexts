{{verse|19|17}}The fourth lot came out for Issachar, even for the children of Issachar according to their families. {{verse|19|18}}And their border was Jezreel, and Chesulloth, and Shunem; {{verse|19|19}}and Hapharaim, and Shion, and Anaharath; {{verse|19|20}}and Rabbith, and Kishion, and Ebez; {{verse|19|21}}and Remeth, and En-gannim, and En-haddah, and Beth-pazzez; {{verse|19|22}}and the border reached to Tabor, and Shahazim, and Beth-shemesh; and the goings out of their border were at the Jordan; sixteen cities with their villages. {{verse|19|23}}This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Issachar according to their families, the cities with their villages.

{{verse|19|24}}And the fifth lot came out for the tribe of the children of Asher according to their families. {{verse|19|25}}And their border was Helkath, and Hali, and Beten, and Achshaph; {{verse|19|26}}and Allammelech, and Amad, and Mishal; and it reached to Carmel westward, and to Shihor-libnath. {{verse|19|27}}And it turned toward the sunrising to Beth-dagon, and reached to Zebulun and to the valley of Iphtahel northward at Beth-emek and Neiel; and it went out to Cabul on the left hand, {{verse|19|28}}and Ebron, and Rehob, and Hammon, and Kanah, even unto great Zidon. {{verse|19|29}}And the border turned to Ramah, and to the fortified city of Tyre; and the border turned to Hosah; and the goings out thereof were at the sea from Hebel to Achzib; {{verse|19|30}}Ummah also, and Aphek, and Rehob; twenty and two cities with their villages. {{verse|19|31}}This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Asher according to their families, these cities with their villages.

{{verse|19|32}}The sixth lot came out for the children of Naphtali, even for the children of Naphtali according to their families. {{verse|19|33}}And their border was from Heleph, from Elon-bezaanannim, and Adami-nekeb, and Jabneel, unto Lakkum; and the goings out thereof were at the Jordan. {{verse|19|34}}And the border turned westward to Aznoth-tabor, and went out from thence to Hukok; and it reached to Zebulun on the south, and reached to Asher on the west, and to Judah at the Jordan toward the sunrising. {{verse|19|35}}And the fortified cities were Ziddim-zer, and Hammath, and Rakkath, and Chinnereth; {{verse|19|36}}and Adamah, and Ramah, and Hazor; {{verse|19|37}}and Kedesh, and Edrei, and En-hazor; {{verse|19|38}}and Iron, and Migdal-el, and Horem, and Beth-anath, and Beth-shemesh; nineteen cities with their villages. {{verse|19|39}}This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Naphtali according to their families, the cities with their villages.

{{verse|19|40}}The seventh lot came out for the tribe of the children of Dan according to their families. {{verse|19|41}}And the border of their inheritance was Zorah, and Eshtaol, and Ir-shemesh; {{verse|19|42}}and Shaalabbin, and Aijalon, and Ithlah; {{verse|19|43}}and Elon, and Timnah, and Ekron; {{verse|19|44}}and Eltekeh, and Gibbethon, and Baalath; {{verse|19|45}}and Jehud, and Bene-berak, and Gath-rimmon; {{verse|19|46}}and Me-jarkon, and Rakkon, with the border over against Joppa. {{verse|19|47}}And the border of the children of Dan was too strait for them; so the children of Dan went up and fought against Leshem, and took it, and smote it with the edge of the sword, and possessed it, and dwelt therein, and called Leshem, Dan, after the name of Dan their father. {{verse|19|48}}This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Dan according to their families, these cities with their villages.
