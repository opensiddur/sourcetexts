{{dropinitial|{{anchor|72:1}}72}} [A Psalm] of Solomon.


:Give the king Thy judgments, O God,
:And Thy righteousness unto the king's son;
:{{verse|72|2|align=float}}That he may judge Thy people with righteousness,
:And Thy poor with justice.
:{{verse|72|3|align=float}}Let the mountains bear peace to the people,
:And the hills, through righteousness.
:{{verse|72|4|align=float}}May he judge the poor of the people,
:And save the children of the needy,
:And crush the oppressor.
:{{verse|72|5|align=float}}They shall fear Thee while the sun endureth,
:And so long as the moon, throughout all generations.
:{{verse|72|6|align=float}}May he come down like rain upon the mown grass,
:As showers that water the earth.
:{{verse|72|7|align=float}}In his days let the righteous flourish,
:And abundance of peace, till the moon be no more.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|72|8|align=float}}May he have dominion also from sea to sea,
:And from the River unto the ends of the earth.
:{{verse|72|9|align=float}}Let them that dwell in the wilderness bow before him;
:And his enemies lick the dust.
:{{verse|72|10|align=float}}The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall render tribute;
:The kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.
:{{verse|72|11|align=float}}Yea, all kings shall prostrate themselves before him;
:All nations shall serve him.
:{{verse|72|12|align=float}}For he will deliver the needy when he crieth; the poor also, and him that hath no helper.
:{{verse|72|13|align=float}}He will have pity on the poor and needy,
:And the souls of the needy he will save.
:{{verse|72|14|align=float}}He will redeem their soul from oppression and violence,
:And precious will their blood be in his sight.
:{{verse|72|15|align=float}}That they may live, and that he may give them of the gold of Sheba,
:That they may pray for him continually,
:Yea, bless him all the day.
:{{verse|72|16|align=float}}May he be as a rich cornfield in the land upon the top of the mountains;
:May his fruit rustle like Lebanon;
:And may they blossom out of the city like grass of the earth.
:{{verse|72|17|align=float}}May his name endure for ever;
:May his name be continued as long as the sun;
:May men also bless themselves by him;
:May all nations call him happy.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|72|18|align=float}}Blessed be the {{sc|Lord}} God, the God of Israel,
:Who only doeth wondrous things;
:{{verse|72|19|align=float}}And blessed be His glorious name for ever;
:And let the whole earth be filled with His glory.
:Amen, and Amen.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|72|20|align=float}}The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.
