<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh||SECOND CHRONICLES|{{larger|23}}.14}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{verse|22|10}}Now when Athaliah the mother of Ahaziah saw that her son was dead, she arose and destroyed all the seed royal of the house of Judah. {{verse|22|11}}But Jehoshabeath, the daughter of the king, took Joash the son of Ahaziah, and stole him away from among the king's sons that were slain, and put him and his nurse in the bed-chamber. So Jehoshabeath, the daughter of king Jehoram, the wife of Jehoiada the priest—for she was the sister of Ahaziah—hid him from Athaliah, so that she slew him not. {{verse|22|12}}And he was with them hid in the house of God six years; and Athaliah reigned over the land.

{{dropinitial|{{anchor|23:1}}23}} And in the seventh year Jehoiada strengthened himself, and took the captains of hundreds, Azariah the son of Jeroham, and Ishmael the son of Jehohanan, and Azariah the son of Obed, and Maaseiah the son of Adaiah, and Elishaphat the son of Zichri, into covenant with him. {{verse|23|2}}And they went about in Judah, and gathered the Levites out of all the cities of Judah, and the heads of fathers' houses of Israel, and they came to Jerusalem. {{verse|23|3}}And all the congregation made a covenant with the king in the house of God. And he said unto them: 'Behold, the king's son shall reign, as the {{sc|Lord}} hath spoken concerning the sons of David. {{verse|23|4}}This is the thing that ye shall do: a third part of you, that come in on the sabbath, of the priests and of the Levites, shall be porters of the doors; {{verse|23|5}}and a third part shall be at the king's house; and a third part at the gate of the foundation; and all the people shall be in the courts of the house of the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|23|6}}But let none come into the house of the {{sc|Lord}}, save the priests, and they that minister of the Levites; they shall come in, for they are holy; but all the people shall keep the charge of the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|23|7}}And the Levites shall compass the king round about, every man with his weapons in his hand; and whosoever cometh into the house, let him be slain; and be ye with the king when he cometh in, and when he goeth out.'

{{verse|23|8}}So the Levites and all Judah did according to all that Jehoiada the priest commanded; and they took every man his men, those that were to come in on the sabbath, with those that were to go out on the sabbath; for Jehoiada the priest dismissed not the courses. {{verse|23|9}}And Jehoiada the priest delivered to the captains of hundreds the spears, and bucklers, and shields, that had been king David's, which were in the house of God. {{verse|23|10}}And he set all the people, every man with his weapon in his hand, from the right side of the house to the left side of the house, along by the altar and the house, by the king round about. {{verse|23|11}}Then they brought out the king's son, and put upon him the crown and the insignia, and made him king; and Jehoiada and his sons anointed him; and they said: 'Long live the king.'

{{verse|23|12}}And when Athaliah heard the noise of the people running and praising the king, she came to the people into the house of the {{sc|Lord}}; {{verse|23|13}}and she looked, and, behold, the king stood on his platform at the entrance, and the captains and the trumpets by the king; and all the people of the land rejoiced, and blew with trumpets; the singers also [played] on instruments of music, and led the singing of praise.
