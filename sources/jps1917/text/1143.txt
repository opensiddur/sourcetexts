{{dropinitial|{{anchor|26:1}}26}} And all the people of Judah took Uzziah, who was sixteen years old, and made him king in the room of his father Amaziah. {{verse|26|2}}He built Eloth, and restored it to Judah, after that the king slept with his fathers. {{verse|26|3}}Sixteen years old was Uzziah when he began to reign; and he reigned fifty and two years in Jerusalem; and his mother's name was Jecoliah of Jerusalem. {{verse|26|4}}And he did that, which was right in the eyes of the {{sc|Lord}}, according to all that his father Amaziah had done. {{verse|26|5}}And he set himself to seek God in the days of Zechariah, who had understanding in the vision of God; and as long as he sought the {{sc|Lord}}, God made him to prosper.
