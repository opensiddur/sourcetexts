{{verse|5|6}}And Solomon had forty thousand stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen. {{verse|5|7}}And those officers provided victual for king Solomon, and for all that came unto king Solomon's table, every man in his month; they let nothing be lacking. {{verse|5|8}}Barley also and straw for the horses and swift steeds brought they unto the place where it should be, every man according to his charge.

{{verse|5|9}}And God gave Solomon wisdom and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the sand that is on the sea-shore. {{verse|5|10}}And Solomon's wisdom excelled the wisdom of all the children of the east, and all the wisdom of Egypt. {{verse|5|11}}For he was wiser than all men: than Ethan the Ezrahite, and Heman, and Calcol, and Darda, the sons of Mahol; and his fame was in all the nations round about. {{verse|5|12}}And he spoke three thousand proverbs; and his songs were a thousand and five. {{verse|5|13}}And he spoke of trees, from the cedar that is in Lebanon even unto the hyssop that springeth out of the wall; he spoke also of beasts, and of fowl, and of creeping things, and of fishes. {{verse|5|14}}And there came of all peoples to hear the wisdom of Solomon, from all kings of the earth, who had heard of his wisdom.

{{verse|5|15}}And Hiram king of Tyre sent his servants unto Solomon; for he had heard that they had anointed him king in the room of his father; for Hiram was ever a lover of David. {{verse|5|16}}And Solomon sent to Hiram, saying: {{verse|5|17}}'Thou knowest how that David my father could not build a house for the name of the {{sc|Lord}} his God for the wars which were about him on every side, until the {{sc|Lord}} put them under the soles of my feet. {{verse|5|18}}But now the {{sc|Lord}} my God hath given me rest on every side; there is neither adversary, nor evil occurrence. {{verse|5|19}}And, behold, I purpose to build a house for the name of the {{sc|Lord}} my God, as the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto David my father, saying: Thy son, whom I will set upon thy throne in thy room, he shall build the house for My name. {{verse|5|20}}Now therefore command thou that they hew me cedar-trees out of Lebanon; and my servants shall be with thy servants; and I will give thee hire for thy servants according to all that thou shalt say; for thou knowest that there is not among us any that hath skill to hew timber like unto the Zidonians.'
