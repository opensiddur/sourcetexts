{{verse|13|15}}And Moses gave unto the tribe of the children of Reuben according to their families. {{verse|13|16}}And their border was from Aroer, that is on the edge of the valley of Arnon, and the city that is in the middle of the valley, and all the table-land by Medeba; {{verse|13|17}}Heshbon, and all her cities that are in the table-land; Dibon, and Bamoth-baal, and Beth-baal-meon; {{verse|13|18}}and Jahaz, and Kedemoth, and Mephaath; {{verse|13|19}}and Kiriathaim, and Sibmah, and Zerethshahar in the mount of the valley; {{verse|13|20}}and Beth-peor, and the slopes of Pisgah, and Beth-jeshimoth; {{verse|13|21}}and all the cities of the table-land, and all the kingdom of Sihon king of the Amorites, who reigned in Heshbon, whom Moses smote with the chiefs of Midian, Evi, and Rekem, and Zur, and Hur, and Reba, the princes of Sihon, that dwelt in the land. {{verse|13|22}}Balaam also the son of Beor, the soothsayer, did the children of Israel slay with the sword among the rest of their slain. {{verse|13|23}}And as for the border of the children of Reuben, the Jordan was their border. This was the inheritance of the children of Reuben according to their families, the cities and the villages thereof.

{{verse|13|24}}And Moses gave unto the tribe of Gad, unto the children of Gad, according to their families. {{verse|13|25}}And their border was Jazer, and all the cities of Gilead, and half the land of the children of Ammon, unto Aroer that is before Rabbah; {{verse|13|26}}and from Heshbon unto Ramath-mizpeh, and Betonim; and from Mahanaim unto the border of Lidbir; {{verse|13|27}}and in the valley, Beth-haram, and Beth-nimrah, and Succoth, and Zaphon, the rest of the kingdom of Sihon king of Heshbon, the Jordan being the border thereof, unto the uttermost part of the sea of Chinnereth beyond the Jordan eastward. {{verse|13|28}}This is the inheritance of the children of Gad according to their families, the cities and the villages thereof.

{{verse|13|29}}And Moses gave inheritance unto the half-tribe of Manasseh; and it was for the half-tribe of the children of Manasseh according to their families. {{verse|13|30}}And their border was from Mahanaim, all Bashan, all the kingdom of Og king of Bashan, and all the villages of Jair, which are in Bashan, threescore cities; {{verse|13|31}}and half Gilead, and Ashtaroth, and Edrei, the cities of the kingdom of Og in Bashan, were for the children of Machir the son of Manasseh, even for the half of the children of Machir according to their families.

{{verse|13|32}}These are the inheritances which Moses distributed in the plains of Moab, beyond the Jordan at Jericho, eastward. {{verse|13|33}}But unto the tribe of Levi Moses gave no inheritance; the {{sc|Lord}}, the God of Israel, is their inheritance, as He spoke unto them.
