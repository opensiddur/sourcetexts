{{dropinitial|{{anchor|10:1}}10}}Hear ye the word which the {{sc|Lord}} speaketh unto you, O house of Israel; {{verse|10|2}}thus saith the {{sc|Lord}}:

:Learn not the way of the nations,
:And be not dismayed at the signs of heaven;
:For the nations are dismayed at them.
:{{verse|10|3|align=float}}For the customs of the peoples are vanity;
:For it is but a tree which one cutteth out of the forest,
:The work of the hands of the workman with the axe.
:{{verse|10|4|align=float}}They deck it with silver and with gold,
:They fasten it with nails and with hammers, that it move not.
:{{verse|10|5|align=float}}They are like a pillar in a garden of cucumbers, and speak not;
:They must needs be borne, because they cannot go.
:Be not afraid of them, for they cannot do evil,
:Neither is it in them to do good.
