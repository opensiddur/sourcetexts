{{verse|45|18}}Thus saith the Lord {{sc|God}}: In the first month, in the first day of the month, thou shalt take a young bullock without blemish; and thou shalt purify the sanctuary. {{verse|45|19}}And the priest shall take of the blood of the sin-offering, and put it upon the door-posts of the house, and upon the four corners of the settle of the altar, and upon the posts of the gate of the inner court. {{verse|45|20}}And so thou shalt do on the seventh day of the month for every one that erreth, and for him that is simple; so shall ye make atonement for the house. {{verse|45|21}}In the first month, in the fourteenth day of the month, ye shall have the passover; a feast of seven days; unleavened bread shall be eaten. {{verse|45|22}}And upon that day shall the prince prepare for himself and for all the people of the land a bullock for a sin-offering. {{verse|45|23}}And the seven days of the feast he shall prepare a burnt-offering to the {{sc|Lord}}, seven bullocks and seven rams without blemish daily the seven days; and a he-goat daily for a sin-offering. {{verse|45|24}}And he shall prepare a meal-offering, an ephah for a bullock, and an ephah for a ram, and a hin of oil to an ephah. {{verse|45|25}}In the seventh month, in the fifteenth day of the month, in the feast, shall he do the like the seven days; to the sin-offering as well as the burnt-offering, and the meal-offering as well as the oil.
