{{verse|40|17}}Then brought he me into the outer court, and, lo, there were chambers and a pavement, made for the court round about; thirty chambers were upon the pavement. {{verse|40|18}}And the pavement was by the side of the gates, corresponding unto the length of the gates, even the lower pavement. {{verse|40|19}}Then he measured the breadth from the forefront of the lower gate unto the forefront of the inner court without, a hundred cubits, eastward as also northward.

{{verse|40|20}}And the gate of the outer court that looked toward the north, he measured the length thereof and the breadth thereof. {{verse|40|21}}And the cells thereof were three on this side and three on that side; and the posts thereof and the arches thereof were after the measure of the first gate; the length thereof was fifty cubits, and the breadth five and twenty cubits. {{verse|40|22}}And the windows thereof, and the arches thereof, and the palm-trees thereof, were after the measure of the gate that looketh toward the east; and it was ascended by seven steps; and the arches thereof were before them. {{verse|40|23}}And there was a gate to the inner court over against the other gate, northward as also eastward; and he measured from gate to gate a hundred cubits.

{{verse|40|24}}And he led me toward the south, and behold a gate toward the south; and he measured the posts thereof, and the arches thereof according to these measures. {{verse|40|25}}And there were windows in it and in the arches thereof round about, like those windows; the length was fifty cubits, and the breadth five and twenty cubits. {{verse|40|26}}And there were seven steps to go up to it, and the arches thereof were before them; and it had palm-trees, one on this side, and another on that side, upon the posts thereof. {{verse|40|27}}And there was a gate to the inner court toward the south; and he measured from gate to gate toward the south a hundred cubits.

{{verse|40|28}}Then he brought me to the inner court by the south gate; and he measured the south gate according to these measures; {{verse|40|29}}and the cells thereof, and the posts thereof, and the arches thereof, according to these measures; and there were windows in it and in the arches thereof round about; it was fifty cubits long, and five and twenty cubits broad. {{verse|40|30}}And there were arches round about, five and twenty cubits long, and five cubits broad. {{verse|40|31}}And the arches thereof were toward the outer court; and palm-trees were upon the posts thereof; and the going up to it had eight steps.
