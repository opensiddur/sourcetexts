{{verse|9|24}}But Pharaoh's daughter came up out of the city of David unto her house which [Solomon] had built for her; then did he build Millo.

{{verse|9|25}}And three times in a year did Solomon offer burnt-offerings and peace-offerings upon the altar which he built unto the {{sc|Lord}}, offering thereby, upon the altar that was before the {{sc|Lord}}. So he finished the house.

{{verse|9|26}}And king Solomon made a navy of ships in Ezion-geber, which is beside Eloth, on the shore of the Red Sea, in the land of Edom. {{verse|9|27}}And Hiram sent in the navy his servants, shipmen that had knowledge of the sea, with the servants of Solomon. {{verse|9|28}}And they came to Ophir, and fetched from thence gold, four hundred and twenty talents, and brought it to king Solomon.

and when she was come to Solomon, she spoke with him of all that was in her heart. {{verse|10|3}}And Solomon told her all her questions; there was not any thing hid from the king which he told her not. {{verse|10|4}}And when the queen of Sheba had seen all the wisdom of Solomon, and the house that he had built, {{verse|10|5}}and the food of his table, and the sitting of his servants, and the attendance of his ministers, and their apparel, and his cupbearers, and his burnt-offering which he offered in the house of the {{sc|Lord}}, there was no more spirit in her. {{verse|10|6}}And she said to the king: 'It was a true report that I heard in mine own land of thine acts, and of thy wisdom. {{verse|10|7}}Howbeit I believed not the words, until I came, and mine eyes had seen it; and, behold, the half was not told me; thou hast wisdom and prosperity exceeding the fame which I heard. {{verse|10|8}}Happy are thy men, happy are these thy servants, that stand continually before thee, and that hear thy wisdom. {{verse|10|9}}Blessed be the {{sc|Lord}} thy God, who delighted in thee, to set thee on the throne of Israel; there came no such sandal-wood, nor was seen, unto this day. {{verse|10|13}}And king Solomon gave to the queen of Sheba all her desire, whatsoever she asked, beside that which Solomon gave her of his royal bounty.
