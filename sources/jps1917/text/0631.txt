{{verse|32|6}}And Jeremiah said: 'The word of the {{sc|Lord}} came unto me, saying: {{verse|32|7}}Behold, Hanamel, the son of Shallum thine uncle, shall come unto thee, saying: Buy thee my field that is in Anathoth; for the right of redemption is thine to buy it.' {{verse|32|8}}So Hanamel mine uncle's son came to me in the court of the guard according to the word of the {{sc|Lord}}, and said unto me: 'Buy my field, I pray thee, that is in Anathoth, which is in the land of Benjamin; for the right of inheritance is thine, and the redemption is thine; buy it for thyself.' Then I knew that this was the word of the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|32|9}}And I bought the field that was in Anathoth of Hanamel mine uncle's son, and weighed him the money, even seventeen shekels of silver. {{verse|32|10}}And I subscribed the deed, and sealed it, and called witnesses, and weighed him the money in the balances. {{verse|32|11}}So I took the deed of the purchase, both that which was sealed, containing the terms and conditions, and that which was open; {{verse|32|12}}and I delivered the deed of the purchase unto Baruch the son of Neriah, the son of Mahseiah, in the presence of Hanamel mine uncle['s son], and in the presence of the witnesses that subscribed the deed of the purchase, before all the Jews that sat in the court of the guard. {{verse|32|13}}And I charged Baruch before them, saying: {{verse|32|14}}'Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel: Take these deeds, this deed of the purchase, both that which is sealed, and this deed which is open, and put them in an earthen vessel; that they may continue many days. {{verse|32|15}}For thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel: Houses and fields and vineyards shall yet again be bought in this land.'
