:{{verse|40|2|align=float}}I waited patiently for the {{sc|Lord}};
:And He inclined unto me, and heard my cry.
:{{verse|40|3|align=float}}He brought me up also out of the tumultuous pit, out of the miry clay;
:And He set my feet upon a rock, He established my goings.
:{{verse|40|4|align=float}}And He hath put a new song in my mouth, even praise unto our God;
:Many shall see, and fear,
:And shall trust in the {{sc|Lord}}.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|40|5|align=float}}Happy is the man that hath made the {{sc|Lord}} his trust,
:And hath not turned unto the arrogant, nor unto such as fall away treacherously.
:{{verse|40|6|align=float}}Many things hast Thou done, O {{sc|Lord}} my God,
:Even Thy wonderful works, and Thy thoughts toward us;
:There is none to be compared unto Thee!
:If I would declare and speak of them,
:They are more than can be told.
:{{verse|40|7|align=float}}Sacrifice and meal-offering Thou hast no delight in;
:Mine ears hast Thou opened;
:Burnt-offering and sin-offering hast Thou not required.
:{{verse|40|8|align=float}}Then said I: 'Lo, I am come
:With the roll of a book which is prescribed for me;
:{{verse|40|9|align=float}}I delight to do Thy will, O my God;
:Yea, Thy law is in my inmost parts.'
:{{verse|40|10|align=float}}I have preached righteousness in the great congregation,
:Lo, I did not refrain my lips;
:O {{sc|Lord}}, Thou knowest.
:{{verse|40|11|align=float}}I have not hid Thy righteousness within my heart;
:I have declared Thy faithfulness and Thy salvation;
:I have not concealed Thy mercy and Thy truth from the great congregation.
:{{verse|40|12|align=float}}Thou, O {{sc|Lord}}, wilt not withhold Thy compassions from me;
:Let Thy mercy and Thy truth continually preserve me.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|40|13|align=float}}For innumerable evils have compassed me about,
:Mine iniquities have overtaken me, so that I am not able to look up;
:They are more than the hairs of my head, and my heart hath failed me.
:{{verse|40|14|align=float}}Be pleased, O {{sc|Lord}}, to deliver me;
:O {{sc|Lord}}, make haste to help me.
:{{verse|40|15|align=float}}Let them be ashamed and abashed together
:That seek after my soul to sweep it away;
:Let them be turned backward and brought to confusion
:That delight in my hurt.
:{{verse|40|16|align=float}}Let them be appalled by reason of their shame
:That say unto me: 'Aha, aha.'
:{{verse|40|17|align=float}}Let all those that seek Thee rejoice and be glad in Thee;
:Let such as love Thy salvation say continually:
:'The {{sc|Lord}} be magnified.'
:{{verse|40|18|align=float}}But, as for me, that am poor and needy,
:The Lord will account it unto me;
:Thou art my help and my deliverer;
:O my God, tarry not.
