{{dropinitial|{{anchor|19:1}}19}}When the {{sc|Lord}} thy God shall cut off the nations, whose land the {{sc|Lord}} thy God giveth thee, and thou dost succeed them, and dwell in their cities, and in their houses; {{verse|19|2}}thou shalt separate three cities for thee in the midst of thy land, which the {{sc|Lord}} thy {{sc|God}} giveth thee to possess it. {{verse|19|3}}Thou shalt prepare thee the way, and divide the borders of thy land, which the {{sc|Lord}} thy God causeth thee to inherit, into three parts, that every manslayer may flee thither. {{verse|19|4}}And this is the case of the manslayer, that shall flee thither and live: whoso killeth his neighbour unawares, and hated him not in time past; {{verse|19|5}}as when a man goeth into the forest with his neighbour to hew wood, and his hand fetcheth a stroke with the axe to cut down the tree, and the head slippeth from the helve, and lighteth upon his neighbour, that he die; he shall flee unto one of these cities and live; {{verse|19|6}}lest the avenger of blood pursue the manslayer, while his heart is hot, and overtake him, because the way is long, and smite him mortally; whereas he was not deserving of death, inasmuch as he hated him not in time past. {{verse|19|7}}Wherefore I command thee, saying: 'Thou shalt separate three cities for thee.' {{verse|19|8}}And if the {{sc|Lord}} thy God enlarge thy border, as He hath sworn unto thy fathers, and give thee all the land which He promised to give unto thy fathers—{{verse|19|9}}if thou shalt keep all this commandment to do it, which I command thee this day, to love the {{sc|Lord}} thy God, and to walk ever in His ways—then shalt thou add three cities more for thee, beside these three; {{verse|19|10}}that innocent blood be not shed in the midst of thy land, which the {{sc|Lord}} thy God giveth thee for an inheritance, and so blood be upon thee.
