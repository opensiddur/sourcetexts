{{verse|23|11}}And after him was Shammah the son of Age the Ararite. And the Philistines were gathered together into a troop, where was a plot of ground full of lentils; and the people fled from the Philistines. {{verse|23|12}}But he stood in the midst of the plot, and defended it, and slew the Philistines; and the {{sc|Lord}} wrought a great victory.

{{verse|23|13}}And three of the thirty chief went down, and came to David in the harvest time unto the cave of Adullam; and the troop of the Philistines were encamped in the valley of Rephaim. {{verse|23|14}}And David was then in the stronghold, and the garrison of the Philistines was then in Beth-lehem. {{verse|23|15}}And David longed, and said: 'Oh that one would give me water to drink of the well of Beth-lehem, which is by the gate! ' {{verse|23|16}}And the three mighty men broke through the host of the Philistines, and drew water out of the well of Beth-lehem, that was by the gate, and took it, and brought it to David; but he would not drink thereof, but poured it out unto the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|23|17}}And he said: 'Be it far from me, O {{sc|Lord}}, that I should do this; shall I drink the blood of the men that went in jeopardy of their lives?' therefore he would not drink it. These things did the three mighty men.

{{verse|23|18}}And Abishai, the brother of Joab, the son of Zeruiah, was chief of the three. And he lifted up his spear against three hundred and slew them, and had a name among the three. {{verse|23|19}}He was most honourable of the three; therefore he was made their captain; howbeit he attained not unto the first three.

{{verse|23|20}}And Benaiah the son of Jehoiada, the son of a valiant man of Kabzeel, who had done mighty deeds, he smote the two altar-hearths of Moab; he went down also and slew a lion in the midst of a pit in time of snow; {{verse|23|21}}and he slew an Egyptian, a goodly man; and the Egyptian had a spear in his hand; but he went down to him with a staff, and plucked the spear out of the Egyptian's hand, and slew him with his own spear. {{verse|23|22}}These things did Benaiah the son of Jehoiada, and had a name among the three mighty men. {{verse|23|23}}He was more honourable than the thirty, but he attained not to the first three. And David set him over his guard.
