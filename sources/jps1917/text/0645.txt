{{dropinitial|{{anchor|43:1}}43}}And it came to pass, that when Jeremiah had made an end of speaking unto all the people all the words of the {{sc|Lord}} their God, wherewith the {{sc|Lord}} their God had sent him to them, even all these words, {{verse|43|2}}then spoke Azariah the son of Hoshaiah, and Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the proud men, saying unto Jeremiah: 'Thou speakest falsely; the {{sc|Lord}} our God hath not sent thee to say: Ye shall not go into Egypt to sojourn there; {{verse|43|3}}but Baruch the son of Neriah setteth thee on against us, to deliver us into the hand of the Chaldeans, that they may put us to death, and carry us away captives to Babylon.' {{verse|43|4}}So Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the captains of the forces, and all the people, hearkened not to the voice of the {{sc|Lord}}, to dwell in the land of Judah. {{verse|43|5}}But Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the captains of the forces, took all the remnant of Judah, that were returned from all the nations whither they had been driven to sojourn in the land of Judah: {{verse|43|6}}the men, and the women, and the children, and the king's daughters, and every person that Nebuzaradan the captain of the guard had left with Gedaliah the son of Ahikam, the son of Shaphan, and Jeremiah the prophet, and Baruch the son of Neriah; {{verse|43|7}}and they came into the land of Egypt; for they hearkened not to the voice of the {{sc|Lord}}; and they came even to Tahpanhes.
