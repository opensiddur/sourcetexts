{{verse|chapter=15|verse=19}}And if a woman have an issue, and her issue in her flesh be blood, she shall be in her impurity seven days; and whosoever toucheth her shall be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=20}}And every thing that she lieth upon in her impurity shall be unclean; every thing also that she sitteth upon shall be unclean. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=21}}And whosoever toucheth her bed shall wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=22}}And whosoever toucheth any thing that she sitteth upon shall wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=23}}And if he be on the bed, or on any thing whereon she sitteth, when he toucheth it, he shall be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=24}}And if any man lie with her, and her impurity be upon him, he shall be unclean seven days; and every bed whereon he lieth shall be unclean.

{{verse|chapter=15|verse=25}}And if a woman have an issue of her blood many days not in the time of her impurity, or if she have an issue beyond the time of her impurity; all the days of the issue of her uncleanness she shall be as in the days of her impurity: she is unclean. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=26}}Every bed whereon she lieth all the days of her issue shall be unto her as the bed of her impurity; and every thing whereon she sitteth shall be unclean, as the uncleanness of her impurity. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=27}}And whosoever toucheth those things shall be unclean, and shall wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=28}}But if she be cleansed of her issue, then she shall number to herself seven days, and after that she shall be clean. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=29}}And on the eighth day she shall take unto her two turtle-doves, or two young pigeons, and bring them unto the priest, to the door of the tent of meeting. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=30}}And the priest shall offer the one for a sin-offering, and the other for a burnt-offering; and the priest shall make atonement for her before the {{sc|Lord}} for the issue of her uncleanness.

{{verse|chapter=15|verse=31}}Thus shall ye separate the children of Israel from their uncleanness; that they die not in their uncleanness, when they defile My tabernacle that is in the midst of them.

{{verse|chapter=15|verse=32}}This is the law of him that hath an issue, and of him from whom the flow of seed goeth out, so that he is unclean thereby; {{verse|chapter=15|verse=33}}and of her that is sick with her impurity, and of them that have an issue, whether it be a man, or a woman; and of him that lieth with her that is unclean.
