{{dropinitial|{{anchor|7:1}}7}}The word that came to Jeremiah from the {{sc|Lord}}, saying: {{verse|7|2}}Stand in the gate of the {{sc|Lord's}} house, and proclaim there this word, and say: Hear the word of the {{sc|Lord}}, all ye of Judah, that enter in at these gates to worship the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|7|3}}Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel:

Amend your ways and your doings, and I will cause you to dwell in this place. {{verse|7|4}}Trust ye not in lying words, saying: 'The temple of the {{sc|Lord}}, the temple of the {{sc|Lord}}, the temple of the {{sc|Lord}}, are these.' {{verse|7|5}}Nay, but if ye thoroughly amend your ways and your doings; if ye thoroughly execute justice between a man and his neighbour; {{verse|7|6}}if ye oppress not the stranger, the fatherless, and the widow, and shed not innocent blood in this place, neither walk after other gods to your hurt; {{verse|7|7}}then will I cause you to dwell in this place, in the land that I gave to your fathers, for ever and ever. {{verse|7|8}}Behold, ye trust in lying words, that cannot profit. {{verse|7|9}}Will ye steal, murder, and commit adultery, and swear falsely, and offer unto Baal, and walk after other gods whom ye have not known, {{verse|7|10}}and come and stand before Me in this house, whereupon My name is called, and say: 'We are delivered', that ye may do all these abominations? {{verse|7|11}}Is this house, whereupon My name is called, become a den of robbers in your eyes? Behold, I, even I, have seen it, saith the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|7|12}}For go ye now unto My place which was in Shiloh, where I caused My name to dwell at the first, and see what I did to it for the wickedness of My people Israel. {{verse|7|13}}And now, because ye have done all these works, saith the {{sc|Lord}}, and I spoke unto you, speaking betimes and often, but ye heard not, and I called you, but ye answered not; {{verse|7|14}}therefore will I do unto the house, whereupon My name is called, wherein ye trust, and unto the place which I gave to you and to your fathers, as I have done to Shiloh. {{verse|7|15}}And I will cast you out of My sight, as I have cast out all your brethren, even the whole seed of Ephraim.
