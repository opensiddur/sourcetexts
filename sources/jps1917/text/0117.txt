{{verse|chapter=25|verse=23}}And thou shalt make a table of acacia-wood: two cubits shall be the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=24}}And thou shalt overlay it with pure gold, and make thereto a crown of gold round about. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=25}}And thou shalt make unto it a border of a handbreadth round about, and thou shalt make a golden crown to the border thereof round about. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=26}}And thou shalt make for it four rings of gold, and put the rings in the four corners that are on the four feet thereof. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=27}}Close by the border shall the rings be, for places for the staves to bear the table. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=28}}And thou shalt make the staves of acacia-wood, and overlay them with gold, that the table may be borne with them. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=29}}And thou shalt make the dishes thereof, and the pans thereof, and the jars thereof, and the bowls thereof, wherewith to pour out; of pure gold shalt thou make them. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=30}}And thou shalt set upon the table showbread before Me alway.

{{verse|chapter=25|verse=31}}And thou shalt make a candlestick of pure gold: of beaten work shall the candlestick be made, even its base, and its shaft; its cups, its knops, and its flowers, shall be of one piece with it. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=32}}And there shall be six branches going out of the sides thereof: three branches of the candlestick out of the one side thereof, and three branches of the candlestick out of the other side thereof; {{verse|chapter=25|verse=33}}three cups made like almond-blossoms in one branch, a knop and a flower; and three cups made like almond-blossoms in the other branch, a knop and a flower; so for the six branches going out of the candlestick. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=34}}And in the candlestick four cups made like almond-blossoms, the knops thereof, and the flowers thereof. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=35}}And a knop under two branches of one piece with it, and a knop under two branches of one piece with it, and a knop under two branches of one piece with it, for the six branches going out of the candlestick. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=36}}Their knops and their branches shall be of one piece with it; the whole of it one beaten work of pure gold. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=37}}And thou shalt make the lamps thereof, seven; and they shall light the lamps thereof, to give light over against it. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=38}}And the tongs thereof, and the snuffdishes thereof, shall be of pure gold. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=39}}Of a talent of pure gold shall it be made, with all these vessels. {{verse|chapter=25|verse=40}}And see that thou make them after their pattern, which is being shown thee in the mount.
