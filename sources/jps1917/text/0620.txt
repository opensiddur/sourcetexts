<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|25}}.9|JEREMIAH|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{verse|25|10}}Moreover I will cause to cease from among them the voice of mirth and the voice of gladness, the voice of the bridegroom and the voice of the bride, the sound of the millstones, and the light of the lamp. {{verse|25|11}}And this whole land shall be a desolation, and a waste; and these nations shall serve the king of Babylon seventy years. {{verse|25|12}}And it shall come to pass, when seventy years are accomplished, that I will punish the king of Babylon, and that nation, saith the {{sc|Lord}}, for their iniquity, and the land of the Chaldeans; and I will make it perpetual desolations. {{verse|25|13}}And I will bring upon that land all My words which I have pronounced against it, even all that is written in this book, which Jeremiah hath prophesied against all the nations. {{verse|25|14}}For many nations and great kings shall make bondmen of them also; and I will recompense them according to their deeds, and according to the work of their own hands.

{{verse|25|15}}For thus saith the {{sc|Lord}}, the God of Israel, unto me: Take this cup of the wine of fury at My hand, and cause all the nations, to whom I send thee, to drink it. {{verse|25|16}}And they shall drink, and reel to and fro, and be like madmen, because of the sword that I will send among them.—{{verse|25|17}}Then took I the cup of the {{sc|Lord's}} hand, and made all the nations to drink, unto whom the {{sc|Lord}} had sent me: {{verse|25|18}}Jerusalem, and the cities of Judah, and the kings thereof, and the princes thereof, to make them an appalment, an astonishment, a hissing, and a curse; as it is this day; {{verse|25|19}}Pharaoh king of Egypt, and his servants, and his princes, and all his people; {{verse|25|20}}and all the mingled people; and all the kings of the land of Uz, and all the kings of the land of the Philistines, and Ashkelon, and Gaza, and Ekron, and the remnant of Ashdod; {{verse|25|21}}Edom, and Moab, and the children of Ammon; {{verse|25|22}}and all the kings of Tyre, and all the kings of Zidon, and the kings of the isle which is beyond the sea; {{verse|25|23}}Dedan, and Tema, and Buz, and all that have the corners of their hair polled; {{verse|25|24}}and all the kings of Arabia, and all the kings of the mingled people that dwell in the wilderness; {{verse|25|25}}and all the kings of Zimri, and all the kings of Elam, and all the kings of the Medes; {{verse|25|26}}and all the kings of the north, far and near, one with another; and all the kingdoms of the world, which are upon the face of the earth.—And the king of <ref>According to ancient tradition, a cipher for ''Babel.''</ref>Sheshach shall drink after them. {{verse|25|27}}And thou shalt say unto them: Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel: Drink ye, and be drunken, and spew, and fall, and rise no more, because of the sword which I will send among you. {{verse|25|28}}And it shall be, if they refuse to take the cup at thy hand to drink, then shalt thou say unto them: Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts: Ye shall surely drink. {{verse|25|29}}For, lo, I begin to bring evil on the city whereupon My name is called, and should ye be utterly unpunished? Ye shall not be unpunished; for I will call for a sword upon all the inhabitants of the earth, saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts.
