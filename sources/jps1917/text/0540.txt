:{{verse|34|6|align=float}}The sword of the {{sc|Lord}} is filled with blood,
:It is made fat with fatness,
:With the blood of lambs and goats,
:With the fat of the kidneys of rams;
:For the {{sc|Lord}} hath a sacrifice in Bozrah,
:And a great slaughter in the land of Edom.
:{{verse|34|7|align=float}}And the wild-oxen shall come down with them,
:And the bullocks with the bulls;
:And their land shall be drunken with blood,
:And their dust made fat with fatness.
:{{verse|34|8|align=float}}For the {{sc|Lord}} hath a day of vengeance,
:A year of recompense for the controversy of Zion.
:{{verse|34|9|align=float}}And the streams thereof shall be turned into pitch,
:And the dust thereof into brimstone,
:And the land thereof shall become burning pitch.
:{{verse|34|10|align=float}}It shall not be quenched night nor day, 
:The smoke thereof shall go up for ever;
:From generation to generation it shall lie waste:
:None shall pass through it for ever and ever.
:{{verse|34|11|align=float}}But the pelican and the bittern shall possess it,
:And the owl and the raven shall dwell therein;
:And He shall stretch over it
:The line of confusion, and the plummet of emptiness.
:{{verse|34|12|align=float}}As for her nobles, none shall be there to be called to the kingdom;
:And all her princes shall be nothing.
:{{verse|34|13|align=float}}And thorns shall come up in her palaces,
:Nettles and thistles in the fortresses thereof;
:And it shall be a habitation of wild-dogs,
:An enclosure for ostriches.
:{{verse|34|14|align=float}}And the wild-cats shall meet with the jackals,
:And the satyr shall cry to his fellow;
:Yea, the night-monster shall repose there,
:And shall find her a place of rest.
:{{verse|34|15|align=float}}There shall the arrowsnake make her nest, and lay,
:And hatch, and brood under her shadow;
:Yea, there shall the kites be gathered,
:Every one with her mate.
:{{verse|34|16|align=float}}Seek ye out of the book of the {{sc|Lord}}, and read;
:No one of these shall be missing,
:None shall want her mate;
:For My mouth it hath commanded,
:And the breath thereof it hath gathered them.
:{{verse|34|17|align=float}}And He hath cast the lot for them,
:And His hand hath divided it unto them by line;
:They shall possess it for ever,
:From generation to generation shall they dwell therein.
