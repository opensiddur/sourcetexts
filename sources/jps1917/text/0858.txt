{{dropinitial|{{anchor|83:1}}83}} A Song, a Psalm of Asaph.


:{{verse|83|2|align=float}}O God, keep not Thou silence;
:Hold not Thy peace, and be not still, O God.
:{{verse|83|3|align=float}}For, lo, Thine enemies are in an uproar;
:And they that hate Thee have lifted up the head.
:{{verse|83|4|align=float}}They hold crafty converse against Thy people,
:And take counsel against Thy treasured ones.
:{{verse|83|5|align=float}}They have said: 'Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation;
:That the name of Israel may be no more in remembrance.'
:{{verse|83|6|align=float}}For they have consulted together with one consent;
:Against Thee do they make a covenant;
:{{verse|83|7|align=float}}The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites;
:Moab, and the Hagrites;
:{{verse|83|8|align=float}}Gebal, and Ammon, and Amalek;
:Philistia with the inhabitants of Tyre;
:{{verse|83|9|align=float}}Assyria also is joined with them;
:They have been an arm to the children of Lot. {{float right|Selah}}
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|83|10|align=float}}Do Thou unto them as unto Midian;
:As to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the brook Kishon;
:{{verse|83|11|align=float}}Who were destroyed at En-dor;
:They became as dung for the earth.
:{{verse|83|12|align=float}}Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb,
:And like Zebah and Zalmunna all their princes;
:{{verse|83|13|align=float}}Who said: 'Let us take to ourselves in possession
:The habitations of God.'
:{{verse|83|14|align=float}}O my God, make them like the whirling dust;
:As stubble before the wind.
:{{verse|83|15|align=float}}As the fire that burneth the forest,
:And as the flame that setteth the mountains ablaze;
:{{verse|83|16|align=float}}So pursue them with Thy tempest,
:And affright them with Thy storm.
:{{verse|83|17|align=float}}Fill their faces with shame;
:That they may seek Thy name, O LORD.
:{{verse|83|18|align=float}}Let them be ashamed and affrighted for ever;
:Yea, let them be abashed and perish;
:{{verse|83|19|align=float}}That they may know that it is Thou alone whose name is the LORD,
:The Most High over all the earth.
