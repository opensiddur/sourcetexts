{{verse|1|28}}Then king David answered and said: 'Call me Bath-sheba.' And she came into the king's presence, and stood before the king. {{verse|1|29}}And the king swore and said: 'As the {{sc|Lord}} liveth, who hath redeemed my soul out of all adversity, {{verse|1|30}}verily as I swore unto thee by the {{sc|Lord}}, the God of Israel, saying: Assuredly Solomon thy son shall reign after me, and he shall sit upon my throne in my stead; verily so will I do this day.' {{verse|1|31}}Then Bath-sheba bowed with her face to the earth, and prostrated herself to the king, and said: 'Let my lord king David live for ever.'

{{verse|1|32}}And king David said: 'Call me Zadok the priest, and Nathan the prophet, and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada.' And they came before the king. {{verse|1|33}}And the king said unto them: 'Take with you the servants of your lord, and cause Solomon my son to ride upon mine own mule, and bring him down to Gihon. {{verse|1|34}}And let Zadok the priest and Nathan the prophet anoint him there king over Israel; and blow ye with the horn, and say: Long live king Solomon. {{verse|1|35}}Then ye shall come up after him, and he shall come and sit upon my throne; for he shall be king in my stead; and I have appointed him to be prince over Israel and over Judah.' {{verse|1|36}}And Benaiah the son of Jehoiada answered the king, and said: 'Amen; so say the {{sc|Lord}}, the God of my lord the king. {{verse|1|37}}As the {{sc|Lord}} hath been with my lord the king, even so be He with Solomon, and make his throne greater than the throne of my lord king David.'

{{verse|1|38}}So Zadok the priest, and Nathan the prophet, and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada, and the Cherethites and the Pelethites, went down, and caused Solomon to ride upon king David's mule, and brought him to Gihon. {{verse|1|39}}And Zadok the priest took the horn of oil out of the Tent, and anointed Solomon. And they blew the ram's horn; and all the people said: 'Long live king Solomon.' {{verse|1|40}}And all the people came up after him, and the people piped with pipes, and rejoiced with great joy, so that the earth rent with the sound of them.
