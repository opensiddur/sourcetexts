{{dropinitial|{{anchor|5:1}}5}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=5|verse=2}}'Command the children of Israel, that they put out of the camp every leper, and every one that hath an issue, and whosoever is unclean by the dead; {{verse|chapter=5|verse=3}}both male and female shall ye put out, without the camp shall ye put them; that they defile not their camp, in the midst whereof I dwell.' {{verse|chapter=5|verse=4}}And the children of Israel did so, and put them out without the camp; as the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, so did the children of Israel.


{{verse|chapter=5|verse=5}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=5|verse=6}}Speak unto the children of Israel:

When a man or woman shall commit any sin that men commit, to commit a trespass against the {{sc|Lord}}, and that soul be guilty; {{verse|chapter=5|verse=7}}then they shall confess their sin which they have done; and he shall make restitution for his guilt in full, and add unto it the fifth part thereof, and give it unto him in respect of whom he hath been guilty. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=8}}But if the man have no kinsman to whom restitution may be made for the guilt, the restitution for guilt which is made shall be the {{sc|Lord}}'s, even the priest's; besides the ram of the atonement, whereby atonement shall be made for him. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=9}}And every heave-offering of all the holy things of the children of Israel, which they present unto the priest, shall be his. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=10}}And every man's hallowed things shall be his: whatsoever any man giveth the priest, it shall be his.
