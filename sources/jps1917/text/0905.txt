:To comprehend the words of understanding;
:{{verse|1|3|align=float}}To receive the discipline of wisdom,

:{{verse|1|4|align=float}}To give prudence to the simple,
:To the young man knowledge and discretion;
:{{verse|1|5|align=float}}That the wise man may hear, and increase in learning,
:And the man of understanding may attain unto wise counsels;

:{{verse|1|7|align=float}}The fear of the {{sc|Lord}} is the beginning of knowledge;
:But the foolish despise wisdom and discipline.

:{{verse|1|8|align=float}}Hear, my son, the instruction of thy father,
:And forsake not the teaching of thy mother;
:{{verse|1|9|align=float}}For they shall be a chaplet of grace unto thy head,
:And chains about thy neck.
{{dhr|50%}}
:{{verse|1|10|align=float}}My son, if sinners entice thee,
:Consent thou not.
:{{verse|1|11|align=float}}If they say: 'Come with us,
:Let us lie in wait for blood,
:Let us lurk for the innocent without cause;
:{{verse|1|12|align=float}}Let us swallow them up alive as the grave,
:And whole, as those that go down into the pit;
:{{verse|1|13|align=float}}We shall find all precious substance,
:We shall fill our houses with spoil;
:{{verse|1|14|align=float}}Cast in thy lot among us;
:Let us all have one purse'—
:{{verse|1|15|align=float}}My son, walk not thou in the way with them,
:Restrain thy foot from their path;
:{{verse|1|16|align=float}}For their feet run to evil,
:And they make haste to shed blood.
:{{verse|1|17|align=float}}For in vain the net is spread
:In the eyes of any bird;
:{{verse|1|18|align=float}}And these lie in wait for their own blood,
:They lurk for their own lives.
:{{verse|1|19|align=float}}So are the ways of every one that is greedy of gain;
:It taketh away the life of the owners thereof.
