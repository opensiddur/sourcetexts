{{verse|chapter=30|verse=17}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=30|verse=18}}'Thou shalt also make a laver of brass, and the base thereof of brass, whereat to wash; and thou shalt put it between the tent of meeting and the altar, and thou shalt put water therein. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=19}}And Aaron and his sons shall wash their hands and their feet thereat; {{verse|chapter=30|verse=20}}when they go into the tent of meeting, they shall wash with water, that they die not; or when they come near to the altar to minister, to cause an offering made by fire to smoke unto the {{sc|Lord}}; {{verse|chapter=30|verse=21}}so they shall wash their hands and their feet, that they die not; and it shall be a statute for ever to them, even to him and to his seed throughout their generations.'

{{verse|chapter=30|verse=22}}Moreover the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=30|verse=23}}'Take thou also unto thee the chief spices, of flowing myrrh five hundred shekels, and of sweet cinnamon half so much, even two hundred and fifty, and of sweet calamus two hundred and fifty, {{verse|chapter=30|verse=24}}and of cassia five hundred, after the shekel of the sanctuary, and of olive oil a hin. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=25}}And thou shalt make it a holy anointing oil, a perfume compounded after the art of the perfumer; it shall be a holy anointing oil. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=26}}And thou shalt anoint therewith the tent of meeting, and the ark of the testimony, {{verse|chapter=30|verse=27}}and the table and all the vessels thereof, and the candlestick and the vessels thereof, and the altar of incense, {{verse|chapter=30|verse=28}}and the altar of burnt-offering with all the vessels thereof, and the laver and the base thereof. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=29}}And thou shalt sanctify them, that they may be most holy; whatsoever toucheth them shall be holy. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=30}}And thou shalt anoint Aaron and his sons, and sanctify them, that they may minister unto Me in the priest's office. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=31}}And thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel, saying: This shall be a holy anointing oil unto Me throughout your generations. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=32}}Upon the flesh of man shall it not be poured, neither shall ye make any like it, according to the composition thereof; it is holy, and it shall be holy unto you. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=33}}Whosoever compoundeth any like it, or whosoever putteth any of it upon a stranger, he shall be cut off from his people.'

{{verse|chapter=30|verse=34}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto Moses: 'Take unto thee sweet spices, stacte, and onycha, and galbanum; sweet spices with pure frankincense; of each shall there be a like weight. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=35}}And thou shalt make of it incense, a perfume after the art of the perfumer, seasoned with salt, pure and holy. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=36}}And thou shalt beat some of it very small, and put of it before the testimony in the tent of meeting, where I will meet with thee; it shall be unto you most holy. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=37}}And the incense which thou shalt make, according to the composition thereof ye shall not make for yourselves; it shall be unto thee holy for the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=30|verse=38}}Whosoever shall make like unto that, to smell thereof, he shall be cut off from his people.'
