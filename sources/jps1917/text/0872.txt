<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|104}}.1|PSALMS|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>:{{dropinitial|{{anchor|104:1}}104}}Bless the {{sc|Lord}}, O my soul.
:O {{sc|Lord}} my God, Thou art very great;
:Thou art clothed with glory and majesty.
:{{verse|104|2|align=float}}Who coverest Thyself with light as with a garment,
:Who stretchest out the heavens like a curtain;
:{{verse|104|3|align=float}}Who layest the beams of Thine upper chambers in the waters,
:Who makest the clouds Thy chariot,
:Who walkest upon the wings of the wind;
:{{verse|104|4|align=float}}Who makest winds Thy messengers,
:The flaming fire Thy ministers.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|104|5|align=float}}Who didst establish the earth upon its foundations,
:That it should not be moved for ever and ever;
:{{verse|104|6|align=float}}Thou didst cover it with the deep as with a vesture;
:The waters stood above the mountains.
:{{verse|104|7|align=float}}At Thy rebuke they fled,
:At the voice of Thy thunder they hasted away—
:{{verse|104|8|align=float}}The mountains rose, the valleys sank down—
:Unto the place which Thou hadst founded for them;
:{{verse|104|9|align=float}}Thou didst set a bound which they should not pass over,
:That they might not return to cover the earth.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|104|10|align=float}}Who sendest forth springs into the valleys;
:They run between the mountains;
:{{verse|104|11|align=float}}They give drink to every beast of the field,
:The wild asses quench their thirst.
:{{verse|104|12|align=float}}Beside them dwell the fowl of the heaven,
:From among the branches they sing.
:{{verse|104|13|align=float}}Who waterest the mountains from Thine upper chambers;
:The earth is full of the fruit of Thy works.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|104|14|align=float}}Who causeth the grass to spring up for the cattle,
:And herb for the service of man;
:To bring forth bread out of the earth,
:{{verse|104|15|align=float}}And wine that maketh glad the heart of man,
:Making the face brighter than oil,
:And bread that stayeth man's heart.
:{{verse|104|16|align=float}}The trees of the {{sc|Lord}} have their fill,
:The cedars of Lebanon, which He hath planted;
:{{verse|104|17|align=float}}Wherein the birds make their nests;
:As for the stork, the fir-trees are her house.
:{{verse|104|18|align=float}}The high mountains are for the wild goats;
:The rocks are a refuge for the conies.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|104|19|align=float}}Who appointedst the moon for seasons;
:The sun knoweth his going down.
:{{verse|104|20|align=float}}Thou makest darkness, and it is night,
:Wherein all the beasts of the forest do creep forth.
:{{verse|104|21|align=float}}The young lions roar after their prey,
:And seek their food from God.
:{{verse|104|22|align=float}}The sun ariseth, they slink away,
:And couch in their dens.
:{{verse|104|23|align=float}}Man goeth forth unto his work
:And to his labour until the evening.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|104|24|align=float}}How manifold are Thy works, O {{sc|Lord}}!
:In wisdom hast Thou made them all;
:The earth is full of Thy creatures.
