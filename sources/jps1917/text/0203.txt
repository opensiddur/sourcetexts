{{verse|chapter=15|verse=27}}And if one person sin through error, then he shall offer a she-goat of the first year for a sin-offering. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=28}}And the priest shall make atonement for the soul that erreth, when he sinneth through error, before the {{sc|Lord}}, to make atonement for him; and he shall be forgiven, {{verse|chapter=15|verse=29}}both he that is home-born among the children of Israel, and the stranger that sojourneth among them: ye shall have one law for him that doeth aught in error. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=30}}But the soul that doeth aught with a high hand, whether he be home-born or a stranger, the same blasphemeth the {{sc|Lord}}; and that soul shall be cut off from among his people. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=31}}Because he hath despised the word of the {{sc|Lord}}, and hath broken His commandment; that soul shall utterly be cut off, his iniquity shall be upon him.


{{verse|chapter=15|verse=32}}And while the children of Israel were in the wilderness, they found a man gathering sticks upon the sabbath day. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=33}}And they that found him gathering sticks brought him unto Moses and Aaron, and unto all the congregation. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=34}}And they put him in ward, because it had not been declared what should be done to him. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=35}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto Moses: 'The man shall surely be put to death; all the congregation shall stone him with stones without the camp.' {{verse|chapter=15|verse=36}}And all the congregation brought him without the camp, and stoned him with stones, and he died, as the {{sc|Lord}} commanded Moses.

{{verse|chapter=15|verse=37}}And the {{sc|Lord}} spoke unto Moses, saying: {{verse|chapter=15|verse=38}}'Speak unto the children of Israel, and bid them that they make them throughout their generations fringes in the corners of their garments, and that they put with the fringe of each corner a thread of blue. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=39}}And it shall be unto you for a fringe, that ye may look upon it, and remember all the commandments of the {{sc|Lord}}, and do them; and that ye go not about after your own heart and your own eyes, after which ye use to go astray; {{verse|chapter=15|verse=40}}that ye may remember and do all My commandments, and be holy unto your God. {{verse|chapter=15|verse=41}}I am the {{sc|Lord}} your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, to be your God: I am the {{sc|Lord}} your God.'
