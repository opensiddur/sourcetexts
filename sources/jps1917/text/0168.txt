Whosoever he be of all your seed throughout your generations, that approacheth unto the holy things, which the children of Israel hallow unto the {{sc|Lord}}, having his uncleanness upon him, that soul shall be cut off from before Me: I am the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=4}}What man soever of the seed of Aaron is a leper, or hath an issue, he shall not eat of the holy things, until he be clean. And whoso toucheth any one that is unclean by the dead; or from whomsoever the flow of seed goeth out; {{verse|chapter=22|verse=5}}or whosoever toucheth any swarming thing, whereby he may be made unclean, or a man of whom he may take uncleanness, whatsoever uncleanness he hath; {{verse|chapter=22|verse=6}}the soul that toucheth any such shall be unclean until the even, and shall not eat of the holy things, unless he bathe his flesh in water. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=7}}And when the sun is down, he shall be clean; and afterward he may eat of the holy things, because it is his bread. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=8}}That which dieth of itself, or is torn of beasts, he shall not eat to defile himself therewith: I am the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=9}}They shall therefore keep My charge, lest they bear sin for it, and die therein, if they profane it: I am the {{sc|Lord}} who sanctify them. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=10}}There shall no <ref>That is, one who is not a priest.</ref>common man eat of the holy thing; but there shall no common man eat thereof. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=14}}And if a man eat of the holy thing through error, then he shall put the fifth part thereof unto it, and shall give unto the priest the holy thing. {{verse|chapter=22|verse=15}}And they shall not profane the holy things of the children of Israel, which they set apart unto the {{sc|Lord}}; {{verse|chapter=22|verse=16}}and so cause them to bear the iniquity that bringeth guilt, when they eat their holy things; for I am the {{sc|Lord}} who sanctify them.
