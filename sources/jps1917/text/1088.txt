{{verse|6|62}}Unto the rest [of the Levites], the sons of Merari, were given, out of the tribe of Zebulun, Rimmono with the open land about it, Tabor with the open land about it; {{verse|6|63}}and beyond the Jordan at Jericho, on the east side of the Jordan, were given them, out of the tribe of Reuben, Bezer in the wilderness with the open land about it, and Jahaz with the open land about it, {{verse|6|64}}and Kedemoth with the open land about it, and Mephaath with the open land about it; {{verse|6|65}}and out of the tribe of Gad: Ramoth in Gilead with the open land about it, and Mahanaim with the open land about it, {{verse|6|66}}and Heshbon with the open land about it, and Jazer with the open land about it.
