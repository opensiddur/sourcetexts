{{dropinitial|{{anchor|36:1}}36}}Now these are the generations of Esau—the same is Edom. {{verse|chapter=36|verse=2}}Esau took his wives of the daughters of Canaan; Adah the daughter of Elon the Hittite, and Oholibamah the daughter of Anah, the daughter of Zibeon the Hivite, {{verse|chapter=36|verse=3}}and Basemath Ishmael's daughter, sister of Nebaioth. {{verse|chapter=36|verse=4}}And Adah bore to Esau Eliphaz; and Basemath bore Reuel; {{verse|chapter=36|verse=5}}and Oholibamah bore Jeush, and Jalam, and Korah. These are the sons of Basemath Esau's wife. {{verse|chapter=36|verse=18}}And these are the sons of Oholibamah Esau's wife: the chief of Jeush, the chief of Jalam, the chief of Korah. These are the chiefs that came of Oholibamah the daughter of Anah, Esau's wife. {{verse|chapter=36|verse=19}}These are the sons of Esau, and these are their chiefs; the same is Edom.
