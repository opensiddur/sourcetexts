{{dropinitial|{{anchor|29:1}}29}} A Psalm of David.


:Ascribe unto the {{sc|Lord}}, O ye sons of might,
:Ascribe unto the {{sc|Lord}} glory and strength.
:{{verse|29|2|align=float}}Ascribe unto the {{sc|Lord}} the glory due unto His name;
:Worship the {{sc|Lord}} in the beauty of holiness.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|29|3|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} is upon the waters;
:The God of glory thundereth,
:Even the {{sc|Lord}} upon many waters.
:{{verse|29|4|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} is powerful;
:The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} is full of majesty.
:{{verse|29|5|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} breaketh the cedars;
:Yea, the {{sc|Lord}} breaketh in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
:{{verse|29|6|align=float}}He maketh them also to skip like a calf;
:Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild-ox.
:{{verse|29|7|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} heweth out flames of fire.
:{{verse|29|8|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} shaketh the wilderness;
:The {{sc|Lord}} shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.
:{{verse|29|9|align=float}}The voice of the {{sc|Lord}} maketh the hinds to calve,
:And strippeth the forests bare;
:And in His temple all say: 'Glory.'
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|29|10|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} sat enthroned at the flood;
:Yea, the {{sc|Lord}} sitteth as King for ever.
:{{verse|29|11|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} will give strength unto His people;
:The {{sc|Lord}} will bless his people with peace.
