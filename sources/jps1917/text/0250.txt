{{verse|chapter=11|verse=29}}And it shall come to pass, when the {{sc|Lord}} thy God shall bring thee into the land whither thou goest to possess it, that thou shalt set the blessing upon mount Gerizim, and the curse upon mount Ebal. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=30}}Are they not beyond the Jordan, behind the way of the going down of the sun, in the land of the Canaanites that dwell in the Arabah, over against Gilgal, beside the terebinths of Moreh? {{verse|chapter=11|verse=31}}For ye are to pass over the Jordan to go in to possess the land which the {{sc|Lord}} your God giveth you, and ye shall possess it, and dwell therein. {{verse|chapter=11|verse=32}}And ye shall observe to do all the statutes and the ordinances which I set before you this day.


{{dropinitial|{{anchor|12:1}}12}}These are the statutes and the ordinances, which ye shall observe to do in the land which the {{sc|Lord}}, the God of thy fathers, hath given thee to possess it, all the days that ye live upon the earth. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=2}}Ye shall surely destroy all the places, wherein the nations that ye are to dispossess served their gods, upon the high mountains, and upon the hills, and under every leafy tree. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=3}}And ye shall break down their altars, and dash in pieces their pillars, and burn their Asherim with fire; and ye shall hew down the graven images of their gods; and ye shall destroy their name out of that place. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=4}}Ye shall not do so unto the {{sc|Lord}} your God. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=5}}But unto the place which the {{sc|Lord}} your God shall choose out of all your tribes to put His name there, even unto His habitation shall ye seek, and thither thou shalt come; {{verse|chapter=12|verse=6}}and thither ye shall bring your burnt-offerings, and your sacrifices, and your tithes, and the offering of your hand, and your vows, and your freewill-offerings, and the firstlings of your herd and of your flock; {{verse|chapter=12|verse=7}}and there ye shall eat before the {{sc|Lord}} your God, and ye shall rejoice in all that ye put your hand unto, ye and your households, wherein the {{sc|Lord}} thy God hath blessed thee. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=8}}Ye shall not do after all that we do here this day, every man whatsoever is right in his own eyes; {{verse|chapter=12|verse=9}}for ye are not as yet come to the rest and to the inheritance, which the {{sc|Lord}} your God giveth thee. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=10}}But when ye go over the Jordan, and dwell in the land which the {{sc|Lord}} your God causeth you to inherit, and He giveth you rest from all your enemies round about, so that ye dwell in safety; {{verse|chapter=12|verse=11}}then it shall come to pass that the place which the {{sc|Lord}} your God shall choose to cause His name to dwell there, thither shall ye bring all that I command you: your burnt-offerings, and your sacrifices, your tithes, and the offering of your hand, and all your choice vows which ye vow unto the {{sc|Lord}}. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=12}}And ye shall rejoice before the {{sc|Lord}} your God, ye, and your sons, and your daughters, and your men-servants, and your maid-servants, and the Levite that is within your gates, forasmuch as he hath no portion nor inheritance with you. {{verse|chapter=12|verse=13}}Take heed to thyself that thou offer not thy burnt-offerings in every place that thou seest; {{verse|chapter=12|verse=14}}but in the place which the {{sc|Lord}} shall choose in one of thy tribes, there thou shalt offer thy burnt-offerings, and there thou shalt do all that I command thee.
