<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|102}}.1|PSALMS|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{dropinitial|{{anchor|102:1}}102}} A Prayer of the afflicted, when he fainteth, and poureth out his complaint before the {{sc|Lord}}.

:{{verse|102|2|align=float}}O {{sc|Lord}}, hear my prayer,
:And let my cry come unto Thee.
:{{verse|102|3|align=float}}Hide not Thy face from me in the day of my distress;
:Incline Thine ear unto me;
:In the day when I call answer me speedily.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|102|4|align=float}}For my days are consumed like smoke,
:And my bones are burned as a hearth.
:{{verse|102|5|align=float}}My heart is smitten like grass, and withered;
:For I forget to eat my bread.
:{{verse|102|6|align=float}}By reason of the voice of my sighing
:My bones cleave to my flesh.
:{{verse|102|7|align=float}}I am like a pelican of the wilderness;
:I am become as an owl of the waste places.
:{{verse|102|8|align=float}}I watch, and am become
:Like a sparrow that is alone upon the housetop.
:{{verse|102|9|align=float}}Mine enemies taunt me all the day;
:They that are mad against me do curse by me.
:{{verse|102|10|align=float}}For I have eaten ashes like bread,
:And mingled my drink with weeping,
:{{verse|102|11|align=float}}Because of Thine indignation and Thy wrath;
:For Thou hast taken me up, and cast me away.
:{{verse|102|12|align=float}}My days are like a lengthening shadow;
:And I am withered like grass.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|102|13|align=float}}But Thou, O {{sc|Lord}}, sittest enthroned for ever;
:And Thy name is unto all generations.
:{{verse|102|14|align=float}}Thou wilt arise, and have compassion upon Zion;
:For it is time to be gracious unto her, for the appointed time is come.
:{{verse|102|15|align=float}}For Thy servants take pleasure in her stones,
:And love her dust.
:{{verse|102|16|align=float}}So the nations will fear the name of the {{sc|Lord}},
:And all the kings of the earth Thy glory;
:{{verse|102|17|align=float}}When the {{sc|Lord}} hath built up Zion,
:When He hath appeared in His glory;
:{{verse|102|18|align=float}}When He hath regarded the prayer of the destitute,
:And hath not despised their prayer.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|102|19|align=float}}This shall be written for the generation to come;
:And a people which shall be created shall praise the {{sc|Lord}}.
:{{verse|102|20|align=float}}For He hath looked down from the height of His sanctuary;
:From heaven did the {{sc|Lord}} behold the earth;
:{{verse|102|21|align=float}}To hear the groaning of the prisoner;
:To loose those that are appointed to death;
:{{verse|102|22|align=float}}That men may tell of the name of the {{sc|Lord}} in Zion,
:And His praise in Jerusalem;
:{{verse|102|23|align=float}}When the peoples are gathered together,
:And the kingdoms, to serve the {{sc|Lord}}.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|102|24|align=float}}He weakened my strength in the way;
:He shortened my days.
:{{verse|102|25|align=float}}I say: 'O my God, take me not away in the midst of my days,
:Thou whose years endure throughout all generations.
