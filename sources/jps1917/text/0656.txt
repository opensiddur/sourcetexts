{{verse|49|34}}The word of the {{sc|Lord}} that came to Jeremiah the prophet concerning Elam in the beginning of the reign of Zedekiah king of Judah, saying:

:{{verse|49|35|align=float}}Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts:
:Behold, I will break the bow of Elam
:The chief of their might.
:{{verse|49|36|align=float}}And I will bring against Elam the four winds
:From the four quarters of heaven,
:And will scatter them toward all those winds;
:And there shall be no nation whither the dispersed of Elam shall not come.
:{{verse|49|37|align=float}}And I will cause Elam to be dismayed before their enemies,
:And before them that seek their life;
:And I will bring evil upon them,

:And I will send the sword after them,
:Till I have consumed them;
:{{verse|49|38|align=float}}And I will set My throne in Elam,
:And will destroy from thence king and princes, saith the {{sc|Lord}}.
:{{verse|49|39|align=float}}But it shall come to pass in the end of days,
:That I will bring back the captivity of Elam, saith the {{sc|Lord}}.
