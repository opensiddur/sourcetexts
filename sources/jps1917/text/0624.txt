<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh|{{larger|28}}.1|JEREMIAH|}}{{rule}}</noinclude>that Hananiah the son of Azzur the prophet, who was of Gibeon, spoke unto me in the house of the {{sc|Lord}}, in the presence of the priests and of all the people, saying: {{verse|28|2}}'Thus speaketh the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel, saying: I have broken the yoke of the king of Babylon. {{verse|28|3}}Within two full years will I bring back into this place all the vessels of the {{sc|Lord's}} house, that Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon took away from this place, and carried them to Babylon; {{verse|28|4}}and I will bring back to this place Jeconiah the son of Jehoiakim, king of Judah, with all the captives of Judah, that went to Babylon, saith the {{sc|Lord}}; for I will break the yoke of the king of Babylon.' {{verse|28|5}}Then the prophet Jeremiah said unto the prophet Hananiah in the presence of the priests, and in the presence of all the people that stood in the house of the {{sc|Lord}}, {{verse|28|6}}even the prophet Jeremiah said: 'Amen! the {{sc|Lord}} do so! the {{sc|Lord}} perform thy words which thou hast prophesied, to bring back the vessels of the {{sc|Lord's}} house, and all them that are carried away captive, from Babylon unto this place! {{verse|28|7}}Nevertheless hear thou now this word that I speak in thine ears, and in the ears of all the people: {{verse|28|8}}The prophets that have been before me and before thee of old prophesied against many countries, and against great kingdoms, of war, and of evil, and of pestilence. {{verse|28|9}}The prophet that prophesieth of peace, when the word of the prophet shall come to pass, then shall the prophet be known, that the {{sc|Lord}} hath truly sent him.'

{{verse|28|10}}Then Hananiah the prophet took the bar from off the prophet Jeremiah's neck, and broke it. {{verse|28|11}}And Hananiah spoke in the presence of all the people, saying: 'Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}}: Even so will I break the yoke of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon from off the neck of all the nations within two full years.' And the prophet Jeremiah went his way. {{verse|28|12}}Then the word of the {{sc|Lord}} came unto Jeremiah, after that Hananiah the prophet had broken the bar from off the neck of the prophet Jeremiah, saying: {{verse|28|13}}'Go, and tell Hananiah, saying: Thus saith the {{sc|Lord}}: Thou hast broken the bars of wood; but thou shalt make in their stead bars of iron. {{verse|28|14}}For thus saith the {{sc|Lord}} of hosts, the God of Israel: I have put a yoke of iron upon the neck of all these nations, that they may serve Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon; and they shall serve him; and I have given him the beasts of the field also.' {{verse|28|15}}Then said the prophet Jeremiah unto Hananiah the prophet: 'Hear now, Hananiah; the {{sc|Lord}} hath not sent thee; but thou makest this people to trust in a lie. {{verse|28|16}}'Therefore thus saith the {{sc|Lord}}: Behold, I will send thee away from off the face of the earth; this year thou shalt die, because thou hast spoken perversion against the {{sc|Lord}}.' {{verse|28|17}}So Hananiah the prophet died the same year in the seventh month.
