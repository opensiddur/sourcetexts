{{dropinitial|{{anchor|38:1}}38}} A Psalm of David, to make memorial.


:{{verse|38|2|align=float}}O {{sc|Lord}}, rebuke me not in Thine anger;
:Neither chasten me in Thy wrath.
:{{verse|38|3|align=float}}For Thine arrows are gone deep into me,
:And Thy hand is come down upon me.
:{{verse|38|4|align=float}}There is no soundness in my flesh because of Thine indignation;
:Neither is there any health in my bones because of my sin.
:{{verse|38|5|align=float}}For mine iniquities are gone over my head;
:As a heavy burden they are too heavy for me.
:{{verse|38|6|align=float}}My wounds are noisome, they fester,

:{{verse|38|7|align=float}}I am bent and bowed down greatly;

:{{verse|38|8|align=float}}For my loins are filled with burning;
:And there is no soundness in my flesh.
:{{verse|38|9|align=float}}I am benumbed and sore crushed;
:I groan by reason of the moaning of my heart.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|38|10|align=float}}Lord, all my desire is before Thee;
:And my sighing is not hid from Thee.
:{{verse|38|11|align=float}}My heart fluttereth, my strength faileth me;
:As for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me.
:{{verse|38|12|align=float}}My friends and my companions stand aloof from my plague;
