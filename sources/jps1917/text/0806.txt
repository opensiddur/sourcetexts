{{dropinitial|{{anchor|14:1}}14}} For the Leader. [A Psalm] of David.


:The fool hath said in his heart: 'There is no God';
:They have dealt corruptly, they have done abominably;
:There is none that doeth good.
:{{verse|14|2|align=float}}The {{sc|Lord}} looked forth from heaven upon the children of men,
:To see if there were any man of understanding, that did seek after God.
:{{verse|14|3|align=float}}They are all corrupt, they are together become impure;
:There is none that doeth good, no, not one.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|14|4|align=float}}'Shall not all the workers of iniquity know it,
:Who eat up My people as they eat bread,
:And call not upon the {{sc|Lord}}?'
:{{verse|14|5|align=float}}There are they in great fear;
:For God is with the righteous generation.
:{{verse|14|6|align=float}}Ye would put to shame the counsel of the poor,
:But the {{sc|Lord}} is his refuge.
{{dhr|100%}}
:{{verse|14|7|align=float}}Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion!
:When the {{sc|Lord}} turneth the captivity of His people,
:Let Jacob rejoice, let Israel be glad.


{{dropinitial|{{anchor|15:1}}15}} A Psalm of David.


:{{sc|Lord}}, who shall sojourn in Thy tabernacle?
:Who shall dwell upon Thy holy mountain?
:{{verse|15|2|align=float}}He that walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness,
:And speaketh truth in his heart;
:{{verse|15|3|align=float}}That hath no slander upon his tongue,
:Nor doeth evil to his fellow,
:Nor taketh up a reproach against his neighbour;
:{{verse|15|4|align=float}}In whose eyes a vile person is despised,
:But he honoureth them that fear the {{sc|Lord}};
:He that sweareth to his own hurt, and changeth not;
:{{verse|15|5|align=float}}He that putteth not out his money on interest,
:Nor taketh a bribe against the innocent.
:He that doeth these things shall never be moved.


{{dropinitial|{{anchor|16:1}}16}} Michtam of David.


:Keep me, O God; for I have taken refuge in Thee.
:{{verse|16|2|align=float}}I have said unto the {{sc|Lord}}: 'Thou art my Lord;
:I have no good but in Thee';
:{{verse|16|3|align=float}}As for the holy that are in the earth,
:They are the excellent in whom is all my delight.
:{{verse|16|4|align=float}}Let the idols of them be multiplied that make suit unto another;
:Their drink-offerings of blood will I not offer,
:Nor take their names upon my lips.
:{{verse|16|5|align=float}}O {{sc|Lord}}, the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup,
:Thou maintainest my lot.

:{{verse|16|6|align=float}}The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places;
:Yea, I have a goodly heritage.
:{{verse|16|7|align=float}}I will bless the {{sc|Lord}}, who hath given me counsel;
:Yea, in the night seasons my reins instruct me.
