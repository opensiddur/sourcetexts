{{dropinitial|{{anchor|5:1}}5}}This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made He him; {{verse|chapter=5|verse=2}}male and female created He them, and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=3}}And Adam lived a hundred and thirty years, and begot a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=4}}And the days of Adam after he begot Seth were eight hundred years; and he begot sons and daughters. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=5}}And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years; and he died.

{{verse|chapter=5|verse=6}}And Seth lived a hundred and five years, and begot Enosh. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=7}}And Seth lived after he begot Enosh eight hundred and seven years, and begot sons and daughters. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=8}}And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years; and he died.

{{verse|chapter=5|verse=9}}And Enosh lived ninety years, and begot Kenan. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=10}}And Enosh lived after he begot Kenan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begot sons and daughters. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=11}}And all the days of Enosh were nine hundred and five years; and he died.

{{verse|chapter=5|verse=12}}And Kenan lived seventy years, and begot Mahalalel. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=13}}And Kenan lived after he begot Mahalalel eight hundred and forty years, and begot sons and daughters. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=14}}And all the days of Kenan were nine hundred and ten years; and he died.

{{verse|chapter=5|verse=15}}And Mahalalel lived sixty and five years, and begot Jared. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=16}}And Mahalalel lived after he begot Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begot sons and daughters. {{verse|chapter=5|verse=17}}And all the days of Mahalalel were eight hundred ninety and five years; and he died.
