This is the border of the children of Judah round about according to their families.

{{verse|15|13}}And unto Caleb the son of Jephunneh he gave a portion among the children of Judah, according to the commandment of the {{sc|Lord}} to Joshua, even Kiriath-arba, which Arba was the father of Anak—the same is Hebron. {{verse|15|14}}And Caleb drove out thence the three sons of Anak, Sheshai, and Ahiman, and Talmai, the children of Anak. {{verse|15|15}}And he went up thence against the inhabitants of Debir—now the name of Debir beforetime was Kiriath-sepher. {{verse|15|16}}And Caleb said: 'He that smiteth Kiriath-sepher, and taketh it, to him will I give Achsah my daughter to wife.' {{verse|15|17}}And Othniel the son of Kenaz, the brother of Caleb, took it; and he gave him Achsah his daughter to wife. {{verse|15|18}}And it came to pass, when she came unto him, that she persuaded him to ask of her father a field; and she alighted from off her ass; and Caleb said unto her: 'What wouldest thou?' {{verse|15|19}}And she said: 'Give me a blessing; for that thou hast set me in the <ref>Heb. ''Land of Negeb,'' that is, ''a dry land.''</ref>Southland, give me therefore springs of water.' And he gave her the Upper Springs and the Nether Springs.

{{verse|15|20}}This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Judah according to their families.

{{verse|15|21}}And the cities at the uttermost part of the tribe of the children of Judah toward the border of Edom in the South were Kabzeel, and Eder, and Jagur; {{verse|15|22}}and Kinah, and Dimonah, and Adadah; {{verse|15|23}}and Kedesh, and Hazor, and Ithnan; {{verse|15|24}}Ziph, and Telem, and Bealoth; {{verse|15|25}}and Hazor, and Hadattah, and Kerioth, and Hezron—the same is Hazor; {{verse|15|26}}Amam, and Shema, and Moladah; {{verse|15|27}}and Hazar-gaddah, and Heshmon, and Beth-pelet; {{verse|15|28}}and Hazar-shual, and Beer-sheba, and Biziothiah; {{verse|15|29}}Baalah, and Iim, and Ezem; {{verse|15|30}}and Eltolad, and Chesil, and Hormah; {{verse|15|31}}and Ziklag, and Madmannah, and Sansannah; {{verse|15|32}}and Lebaoth, and Shilhim, and Ain, and Rimmon; all the cities are twenty and nine, with their villages.
