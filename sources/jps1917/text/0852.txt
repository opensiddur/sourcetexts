{{dropinitial|{{anchor|77:1}}77}} For the Leader; for Jeduthun. A Psalm of Asaph.


:{{verse|77|2|align=float}}I will lift up my voice unto God, an cry;
:I will lift up my voice unto God, that He may give ear unto me.
:{{verse|77|3|align=float}}In the day of my trouble I seek the Lord;
:With my hand uplifted, [mine eye] streameth in the night without ceasing;
:My soul refuseth to be comforted.
:{{verse|77|4|align=float}}When I think thereon, O God, I must moan;
:When I muse thereon, my spirit fainteth. {{float right|Selah}}
:{{verse|77|5|align=float}}Thou holdest fast the lids of mine eyes;
:I am troubled, and cannot speak.
:{{verse|77|6|align=float}}I have pondered the days of old, the years of ancient times.
:{{verse|77|7|align=float}}In the night I will call to remembrance my song;
:I will commune with mine own heart;
:And my spirit maketh diligent search
:{{verse|77|8|align=float}}'Will the Lord cast off for ever?
:And will He be favourable no more?
:{{verse|77|9|align=float}}Is His mercy clean gone for ever?
:Is His promise come to an end for evermore?
:{{verse|77|10|align=float}}Hath God forgotten to be gracious?
:Hath He in anger shut up his compassions?' {{float right|Selah}}
:{{verse|77|11|align=float}}And I say 'This is my weakness,
:That the right hand of the Most High could change.
:{{verse|77|12|align=float}}I will make mention of the deeds of the {{sc|Lord}};
:Yea, I will remember Thy wonders of old.
:{{verse|77|13|align=float}}I will meditate also upon all Thy work,
:And muse on Thy doings.'
:{{verse|77|14|align=float}}O God, Thy way is in holiness;
:Who is a great god like unto God?
:{{verse|77|15|align=float}}Thou art the God that doest wonders;
:Thou hast made known Thy strength among the peoples.
:{{verse|77|16|align=float}}Thou hast with Thine arm redeemed Thy people,
:The sons of Jacob and Joseph. {{float right|Selah}}
:{{verse|77|17|align=float}}The waters saw Thee, O God;
:The waters saw Thee, they were in pain;
:The depths also trembled.
:{{verse|77|18|align=float}}The clouds flooded forth waters;
:The skies sent out a sound;
:Thine arrows also went abroad.
:{{verse|77|19|align=float}}The voice of Thy thunder was in the whirlwind;
:The lightnings lighted up the world;
:The earth trembled and shook.
:{{verse|77|20|align=float}}Thy way was in the sea,
:And Thy path in the great waters,
:And Thy footsteps were not known.
:{{verse|77|21|align=float}}Thou didst lead Thy people like a flock,
:By the hand of Moses and Aaron.
