<noinclude><pagequality level="1" user="Prosody" />{{rh||JEREMIAH|{{larger|3}}.21}}{{rule}}</noinclude>{{verse|3|6}}And the {{sc|Lord}} said unto me in the days of Josiah the king: 'Hast thou seen that which backsliding Israel did? she went up upon every high mountain and under every leafy tree, and there played the harlot. {{verse|3|7}}And I said: After she hath done all these things, she will return unto me; but she returned not. And her treacherous sister Judah saw it. {{verse|3|8}}And I saw, when, forasmuch as backsliding Israel had committed adultery, I had put her away and given her a bill of divorcement, that yet treacherous Judah her sister feared not; but she also went and played the harlot; {{verse|3|9}}and it came to pass through the lightness of her harlotry, that the land was polluted, and she committed adultery with stones and with stocks; {{verse|3|10}}and yet for all this her treacherous sister Judah hath not returned unto Me with her whole heart, but feignedly, saith the {{sc|Lord}}—{{verse|3|11}}even the {{sc|Lord}} said unto me—backsliding Israel hath proved herself more righteous than treacherous Judah. {{verse|3|12}}Go, and proclaim these words toward the north, and say:

:Return, thou backsliding Israel,
:Saith the {{sc|Lord}};
:I will not frown upon you;
:For I am merciful, saith the {{sc|Lord}},
:I will not bear grudge for ever.
:{{verse|3|13|align=float}}Only acknowledge thine iniquity,
:That thou hast transgressed against the {{sc|Lord}} thy God,
:And hast scattered thy ways to the strangers
:Under every leafy tree,
:And ye have not hearkened to My voice,

{{verse|3|14}}Return, O backsliding children, saith the {{sc|Lord}}; for I am a lord unto you, and I will take you one of a city, and two of a family, and I will bring you to Zion; {{verse|3|15}}and I will give you shepherds according to My heart, who shall feed you with knowledge and understanding. {{verse|3|16}}And it shall come to pass, when ye are multiplied and increased in the land, in those days, saith the {{sc|Lord}}, they shall say no more: The ark of the covenant of the {{sc|Lord}}; neither shall it come to mind; neither shall they make mention of it; neither shall they miss it; neither shall it be made any more. {{verse|3|17}}At that time they shall call Jerusalem The throne of the {{sc|Lord}}; and all the nations shall be gathered unto it, to the name of the {{sc|Lord}}, to Jerusalem; neither shall they walk any more after the stubbornness of their evil heart. {{verse|3|18}}In those days the house of Judah shall walk with the house of Israel, and they shall come together out of the land of the north to the land that I have given for an inheritance unto your fathers.'
